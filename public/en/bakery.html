ü•ß Gubadiya with Kort

Gubadiya is one of the most iconic symbols of Tatar cuisine. It is a multi-layered festive pie filled with kort (a unique dried cottage cheese, slowly caramelized to an amber color), rice, and raisins. Gubadiya is traditionally served at weddings, Sabantuy celebrations, and other important gatherings. Its sweet-savory flavor profile is unlike anything else in world cuisine.

üçØ Chak-Chak

Chak-chak is the most iconic Tatar dessert and a symbol of Tatar hospitality. Small pieces of dough are deep-fried until golden and then drenched in hot honey, pressed together into a sweet, crunchy mound. Chak-chak is traditionally served at weddings and celebrations as a symbol of joy and abundance. It is the most popular souvenir from Kazan.
