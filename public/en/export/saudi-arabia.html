🇸🇦 Halal Meat Products from Kazan, Russia — Export to Saudi Arabia

Authentic Tatar halal craftsmanship, delivered wholesale to Riyadh, Jeddah and Dammam.

HALAL · B2B · SA

Kazan Delicacies (pepperoni.tatar) is a B2B manufacturer of premium halal meat products based in Kazan, Tatarstan — one of Russia's most established halal food production regions with deep-rooted Islamic culinary traditions. We produce a full range of beef and poultry products including pepperoni, hot-dog sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk (traditional Tatar dried sausage), and authentic Tatar baked goods such as echpochmak and chak-chak — all manufactured under strict halal standards from start to finish.

We supply importers, distributors, retail chains, and HoReCa operators across Saudi Arabia on a wholesale basis, with minimum orders from 500 kg per month. Whether you are sourcing for a pizza chain in Riyadh, a fast-food network in Jeddah, a hotel group in Dammam, or building your own private label line, Kazan Delicacies offers consistent quality, flexible SKU configurations, and a dedicated export team to support your business.

Certification & market access

Products are certified by the Halal Standard Committee of the Muslim Religious Board of Tatarstan (certificate No. 614A/2024). The body holds GSO/GCC accreditation, so the halal certificate is recognized in Saudi Arabia.

The facility is listed in the Rosselkhoznadzor “Cerberus” registry (No. RU-016/SB88273); veterinary terms for Saudi Arabia are confirmed individually per contract.

Saudi Arabia is one of the world's most significant halal food markets, with a large and growing demand for high-quality processed meat products across all channels — modern retail, foodservice, and institutional catering. The rapid expansion of international QSR concepts, homegrown burger and pizza brands, and premium HoReCa venues in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam has created strong and sustained demand for reliable halal pepperoni, specialty sausages, and ready-to-cook burger patties that meet both taste expectations and halal integrity requirements.

Tatar and Central Asian culinary heritage has long been intertwined with Islamic food culture, making Kazan a uniquely credible origin for authentic halal meat products. Our kazylyk and smoked sausages offer Saudi importers a distinctive, story-driven product category that resonates with consumers seeking traditional halal charcuterie alternatives. Tatar baked goods such as echpochmak and chak-chak open additional opportunities in specialty food retail, ethnic food sections, and corporate gifting segments.

Sourcing from Kazan offers Saudi buyers a competitive combination of established halal production culture, modern processing infrastructure, and pricing that remains attractive compared to Western European suppliers. Russia's geographic position and developing freight corridors to the Gulf region allow for practical refrigerated and frozen logistics solutions, making Kazan Delicacies a commercially viable long-term partner for the Saudi market.

How supply works

All shipments from Kazan to Saudi Arabia are arranged via temperature-controlled refrigerated or frozen freight, using road-to-port connections to Black Sea or Caspian ports and onward sea freight to Jeddah Islamic Port or King Abdulaziz Port in Dammam. Our frozen product range carries shelf life that comfortably accommodates transit times on these routes, ensuring product integrity upon arrival. We work with experienced freight forwarders familiar with Gulf import requirements and can coordinate full-pallet and full-container loads depending on order volume. Standard lead time from order confirmation to cargo readiness is discussed individually and depends on product mix and production scheduling.

To initiate an export partnership, we ask prospective Saudi buyers to provide their import license details, preferred product list and monthly volume forecast, and target delivery port. Our export team will prepare a commercial offer including product specifications, packaging options, pricing on EXW or FOB terms, and Private Label feasibility for orders meeting the required monthly threshold. We recommend starting with a trial shipment to validate customs clearance procedures on your side and establish the operational workflow before scaling to regular supply.

  • Delivery terms: EXW Kazan (Incoterms 2020); other terms negotiable.
  • Documents: halal certificate, veterinary certificates, EAEU declarations of conformity.
  • Payment currencies: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN.
  • Range: 77 halal SKUs — pepperoni, sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk, Tatar pastry.
  • Private Label / OEM: production under the customer's brand from 500 kg/month.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for export to Saudi Arabia?

Our standard MOQ for export orders is 500 kg per month. This can be a single SKU or a mixed assortment of products. For Private Label orders, we typically require a higher monthly commitment, which our export team will confirm based on your specific product selection and packaging requirements.

What export documents do you provide with each shipment?

We provide a full standard export documentation package including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, veterinary health certificate issued by the relevant Russian authority, and halal certificate from an accredited Islamic body. We coordinate with your customs broker to ensure all documents meet Saudi Customs and SFDA import requirements.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Saudi Arabia typically take?

Total transit time from production readiness to arrival at a Saudi port generally ranges from three to six weeks depending on the routing, freight schedule, and chosen port of entry. We discuss realistic timelines with each buyer during the quotation stage and plan production accordingly to align with your required delivery window.

Can you produce products under our private label (own brand)?

Yes. We offer full Private Label and co-manufacturing services for qualified partners. This includes custom packaging design support, label printing in Arabic and English, and SKU-level customization of product weight, seasoning profile, and packaging format. Private Label production is available for most product categories starting from an agreed monthly volume. Please contact our export team to discuss your brand requirements and receive a feasibility assessment.
