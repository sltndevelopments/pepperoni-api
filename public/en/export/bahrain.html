🇧🇭 Halal Meat Products Export from Kazan, Russia to Bahrain

Authentic Tatar halal craftsmanship — delivered to Manama and beyond.

HALAL · B2B · BH

Kazan Delicacies (pepperoni.tatar) is a Russian B2B manufacturer of premium halal meat products based in Kazan, Tatarstan — one of Russia's longest-standing centres of Islamic food culture. We produce a full range of halal beef and poultry products: pepperoni, hot-dog sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, traditional kazylyk, and Tatar baked goods including echpochmak and chak-chak.

We supply importers, distributors, retail chains, and HoReCa operators across international markets, including Bahrain. Whether you are sourcing for a supermarket network in Manama, equipping a restaurant group, or building a private-label product line, we offer flexible wholesale terms starting from 500 kg per month with the option to scale to full container volumes.

Certification & market access

Products are certified by the Halal Standard Committee of the Muslim Religious Board of Tatarstan (certificate No. 614A/2024). The body holds GSO/GCC accreditation, so the halal certificate is recognized in Bahrain.

The facility is listed in the Rosselkhoznadzor “Cerberus” registry (No. RU-016/SB88273); veterinary terms for Bahrain are confirmed individually per contract.

Bahrain's food market is defined by a predominantly Muslim consumer base with high expectations for halal integrity and product quality. Demand for convenient, ready-to-cook and ready-to-use halal proteins — from burger patties and sausages in QSR and casual dining to deli-style sliced meats in modern retail — continues to grow steadily across the Kingdom, particularly in Manama's expanding hospitality and food-service sector.

HoReCa operators — hotels, restaurant chains, catering companies, and food courts — represent a strong opportunity for consistent, high-volume halal meat procurement. Pepperoni and smoked sausages are in particular demand among pizza concepts and international cuisine restaurants looking for reliable halal-compliant alternatives to conventional European products. Our kazylyk and Tatar baked goods also open a niche in ethnic food retail and specialty delicatessen formats.

Sourcing from Tatarstan offers Bahraini buyers a meaningful advantage: competitive factory-direct pricing, deep-rooted halal food manufacturing tradition, and production capacity that supports both branded and private-label programmes. Russia's geographic position enables cost-efficient multimodal logistics to Gulf markets, while our experience in export documentation ensures a smooth import process on the Bahraini side.

How supply works

Shipments from Kazan to Bahrain are arranged via refrigerated transport combining road and sea freight, typically routing through Russian Black Sea or Caucasus transit points to Gulf ports, with delivery to Manama accessible through Khalifa Bin Salman Port. Our products — including frozen burger patties, sausages, and smoked items — carry shelf lives that comfortably accommodate international transit times. We work with minimum order quantities from one pallet, with full pallet and container loads available for regular supply programmes.

To initiate a supply relationship, we ask prospective Bahraini partners to share their product requirements, preferred pack formats, and monthly volume estimates. We will prepare a tailored commercial offer including product specifications, pricing on CPT or CIF terms, and a sample shipment plan. Our export team handles all necessary shipping documentation and coordinates directly with your customs broker or freight forwarder to ensure efficient clearance.

  • Delivery terms: EXW Kazan (Incoterms 2020); other terms negotiable.
  • Documents: halal certificate, veterinary certificates, EAEU declarations of conformity.
  • Payment currencies: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN.
  • Range: 77 halal SKUs — pepperoni, sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk, Tatar pastry.
  • Private Label / OEM: production under the customer's brand from 500 kg/month.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity for export to Bahrain?

Our standard minimum order starts at 500 kg per month across one or more product lines. For initial trial orders we can discuss smaller pallet-level shipments to allow you to assess product quality and market reception before committing to larger volumes.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Manama typically take?

Transit time from our facility in Kazan to Bahrain generally ranges from three to five weeks depending on the routing and freight schedule. We plan shipments in advance and keep you informed at each logistics stage. All products are shipped under controlled temperature conditions to maintain quality on arrival.

Is Private Label production available for the Bahraini market?

Yes. We offer full private-label and OEM manufacturing from 500 kg per SKU per month. We can produce under your brand with custom packaging, labelling in English and Arabic, and product formulations adapted to your target segment — whether retail, HoReCa, or food service. Our team will guide you through the artwork and approval process.

What export documents do you provide with each shipment?

Each shipment is accompanied by a standard set of export documents including a commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and veterinary health certificate issued by the relevant Russian authority. We coordinate documentation to meet Bahraini import requirements and can advise on any additional paperwork your customs broker may need.
