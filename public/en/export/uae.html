🇦🇪 Halal Meat Products Export to the United Arab Emirates

From the heart of Tatarstan — authentic halal beef and poultry products for distributors, retail chains and HoReCa in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Sharjah.

HALAL · B2B · AE

Kazan Delicacies (pepperoni.tatar) is a B2B manufacturer of halal meat and bakery products based in Kazan, Republic of Tatarstan, Russia — a region with centuries of deep-rooted halal food culture. We produce a full range of ready-to-sell and ready-to-cook items: halal pepperoni, hot dog sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk (traditional Tatar dried sausage), and authentic Tatar baked goods including echpochmak and chak-chak — all made exclusively from beef and poultry.

We work directly with importers, regional distributors, supermarket chains, and HoReCa operators across the UAE. Whether you are looking to introduce a new halal SKU into your portfolio, source a reliable private-label partner, or diversify your current supplier base, Kazan Delicacies offers consistent quality, competitive wholesale pricing, and flexible minimum order quantities starting from 500 kg per month.

Certification & market access

Products are certified by the Halal Standard Committee of the Muslim Religious Board of Tatarstan (certificate No. 614A/2024). The body holds GSO/GCC accreditation, so the halal certificate is recognized in United Arab Emirates.

The facility is listed in the Rosselkhoznadzor “Cerberus” registry (No. RU-016/SB88273); veterinary terms for United Arab Emirates are confirmed individually per contract.

The United Arab Emirates is one of the world's most dynamic halal food markets. With a predominantly Muslim population and a large international expatriate community that actively seeks certified halal products, demand for high-quality halal processed meats continues to grow across all channels — from premium supermarkets in Dubai and Abu Dhabi to fast-casual restaurant chains and hotel kitchens in Sharjah and beyond.

HoReCa is a particularly strong channel for our product range. Halal pepperoni is in constant demand among pizza concepts and delivery kitchens; burger patties and hot dog sausages are essential for QSR and café operators; smoked and dried sausages appeal to deli counters and specialty food retailers. Tatar bakery items — echpochmak and chak-chak — offer buyers a genuinely differentiated ethnic food proposition with strong shelf appeal and storytelling potential.

Sourcing from Tatarstan combines several practical advantages for UAE buyers: halal manufacturing is not an add-on here — it is the foundational standard of the entire industry. Competitive production costs translate into strong landed margins for importers, while Russia's established cold-chain export infrastructure ensures product integrity from Kazan to UAE ports. Our production volumes allow us to scale with growing demand without compromising lead times or quality consistency.

How supply works

Shipments from Kazan to the UAE are handled via refrigerated (0°C to +4°C) or frozen (−18°C) road and multimodal transport routes to seaports, with onward container shipping to Jebel Ali (Dubai) or Khalifa Port (Abu Dhabi). Minimum shipment starts from one pallet, with full container loads available for larger orders. Our core product lines carry shelf lives that comfortably accommodate standard transit and customs clearance timelines, ensuring your stock arrives in full commercial condition. We coordinate export documentation and can work with your nominated freight forwarder or recommend established logistics partners experienced in Russia–UAE trade lanes.

To initiate a supply relationship, we ask buyers to provide their company registration details, target product list, preferred delivery Incoterms (EXW, FCA, or CIF), and estimated monthly volume. We will prepare a tailored commercial offer, including product specifications, packaging options, and — where applicable — private label development terms. Our export team is available to arrange a product sample shipment prior to the first commercial order so your quality assurance team can evaluate the range firsthand.

  • Delivery terms: EXW Kazan (Incoterms 2020); other terms negotiable.
  • Documents: halal certificate, veterinary certificates, EAEU declarations of conformity.
  • Payment currencies: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN.
  • Range: 77 halal SKUs — pepperoni, sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk, Tatar pastry.
  • Private Label / OEM: production under the customer's brand from 500 kg/month.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for export to the UAE?

Our standard MOQ for export is 500 kg per month across one or more SKUs. For initial trial or sampling orders, we can discuss smaller quantities on a case-by-case basis. Full pallet and full container orders receive preferential pricing tiers.

What export and product documentation do you provide with each shipment?

We supply a full standard export documentation package with every shipment, including a commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, veterinary health certificate issued by the relevant Russian authority, and halal certificate from an accredited Islamic certifying body. We can align documentation formats with your import broker's requirements in advance of the first shipment.

What are typical lead times from order confirmation to delivery in the UAE?

Production lead time after order confirmation is typically 10–15 business days depending on product type and volume. Transit time from Kazan to UAE ports via refrigerated multimodal routing is generally 18–28 days. We recommend planning your first order with a 6–8 week total lead time to allow for customs clearance and any administrative processing on the UAE side.

Do you offer Private Label (own-brand) production for UAE distributors or retail chains?

Yes. We offer full private label manufacturing for distributors, retail chains, and HoReCa brands starting from 500 kg per month per SKU. We can develop custom packaging artwork, labelling in English and Arabic, and product formulations tailored to your target market. Our team will provide a private label development proposal — including timelines and tooling costs — upon receipt of your brand brief and target SKU list.
