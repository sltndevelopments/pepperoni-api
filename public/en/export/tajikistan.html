Authentic Tatar halal flavors — delivered to Dushanbe, Khujand and beyond.

HALAL · B2B · TJ

Kazan Delicacies (pepperoni.tatar) is a B2B manufacturer of premium halal meat products based in Kazan, Tatarstan — the heart of Russia's halal food industry. We produce a full range of beef and poultry products: pepperoni, hot dog sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, traditional kazylyk, and Tatar baked goods including echpochmak and chak-chak, all crafted under strict halal standards.

We supply wholesale to importers, distributors, retail chains, and HoReCa operators across Tajikistan. Whether you are sourcing for supermarkets in Dushanbe, foodservice networks in Khujand, or building a private label brand for the local market, we offer flexible partnership formats starting from 500 kg per month.

Certification & market access

Products carry the halal certificate of the Muslim Board of Tatarstan (No. 614A/2024) with international SMIIC and GSO/GCC accreditation; recognition terms in Tajikistan are confirmed when arranging supply.

The facility (No. RU-016/SB88273) is listed in the Rosselkhoznadzor “Cerberus” registry and attested for export to Tajikistan.

Tajikistan is a predominantly Muslim country with a deeply rooted halal food culture, making it a natural and growing market for high-quality halal meat products. Rising urbanization and the expansion of modern retail and foodservice in cities like Dushanbe and Khujand are creating consistent demand for processed halal meat — from ready-to-cook burger patties and sausages to premium deli items and traditional products.

The HoReCa segment — including pizza restaurants, fast food outlets, hotel kitchens, and catering companies — represents a particularly dynamic niche. Products such as halal pepperoni for pizza and hot dog sausages for quick-service restaurants are seeing growing interest as Western-style food formats gain popularity among urban consumers. Retail chains and wholesale distributors are equally active in seeking reliable, consistent halal supply partners.

Sourcing from Kazan offers Tajikistani buyers a compelling combination of geographic proximity within the CIS trade zone, competitive pricing compared to European or Middle Eastern suppliers, and a shared halal food tradition rooted in centuries of Tatar Muslim heritage. Established trade routes and logistics corridors between Russia and Tajikistan make regular supply both practical and cost-efficient.

How supply works

Shipments from our production facility in Kazan to Tajikistan are carried out by refrigerated transport, maintaining the cold chain throughout the journey. Our products — including sausages, smoked items, and frozen patties — have shelf lives designed for international distribution, ensuring full product integrity upon arrival in Dushanbe or Khujand. We work with experienced freight partners familiar with CIS cross-border logistics and can organize delivery from a single pallet upward, with full accompanying commercial documentation.

To initiate a supply partnership, we ask prospective buyers to share their target product list, required volumes, delivery frequency, and preferred delivery terms (EXW, FCA, or DAP). Our export team will prepare a tailored commercial offer, coordinate sampling if needed, and guide you through the onboarding process. We are ready to discuss Private Label / OEM production for buyers seeking to launch their own branded halal meat line in the Tajikistani market, with a minimum of 500 kg per month per SKU.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for export to Tajikistan?

Our standard MOQ for export orders is 500 kg per month. This can be a single product or a mixed assortment across several SKUs. For Private Label orders, the same minimum applies per individual product line. We are open to discussing trial shipments for new partners to help you assess product performance in your market.

What commercial documents do you provide with each shipment?

Each export shipment is accompanied by a full set of commercial and shipping documents, including a commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, veterinary health certificate, and halal certificate issued by an accredited body. We prepare documentation in accordance with Tajikistani import requirements and can coordinate with your customs broker to ensure a smooth clearance process.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Tajikistan typically take?

Transit time by refrigerated road transport from Kazan to Dushanbe or Khujand typically ranges from 5 to 10 business days depending on routing, border crossing conditions, and the specific delivery point. We work with logistics partners experienced in CIS routes and will provide you with realistic lead time estimates at the quotation stage, including production lead time.

Is Private Label production available, and what does it involve?

Yes, we offer full Private Label (OEM) production for distributors and retail chains wishing to sell our products under their own brand in Tajikistan. We handle product formulation, packaging design adaptation, and labeling in accordance with your requirements. The minimum volume for a Private Label SKU is 500 kg per month. Please contact our export team to discuss your brand concept and we will prepare a dedicated proposal.
