We supply Armenian importers, distributors, retail chains, and HoReCa operators with consistent, high-quality halal products. Whether you are sourcing for a supermarket network in Yerevan, a restaurant group in Gyumri, or building a private label line, we offer flexible wholesale programs starting from 500 kg per month with scalable volumes to match your growth.

Certification & market access

Products carry the halal certificate of the Muslim Board of Tatarstan (No. 614A/2024) with international SMIIC and GSO/GCC accreditation; recognition terms in Armenia are confirmed when arranging supply.

How supply works

Deliveries from Kazan to Armenia are arranged via refrigerated road freight, with transit typically routed through Georgia, ensuring cold chain integrity from our production facility to your warehouse in Yerevan or Gyumri. Our products carry shelf lives that comfortably accommodate international transit times, and we ship in pallet-based quantities to optimize freight costs. Minimum order starts at one pallet, with consolidated shipments available for buyers testing multiple SKUs.

To initiate a supply partnership, we ask buyers to provide basic company details, target product list, and estimated monthly volumes. Our export team prepares a commercial offer including product specifications, pricing on EXW or DAP terms, and lead times within 2 business days. We work with established freight forwarders experienced on the Russia–Armenia corridor and can recommend logistics partners if needed.

  • Delivery terms: EXW Kazan (Incoterms 2020); other terms negotiable.
  • Documents: halal certificate, veterinary certificates, EAEU declarations of conformity.
  • Payment currencies: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN.
  • Range: 77 halal SKUs — pepperoni, sausages, burger patties, smoked sausages, kazylyk, Tatar pastry.

FAQ

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for export to Armenia?

Our standard MOQ for export is 500 kg per month across your selected product range. For initial trial orders, we can discuss smaller pallet-level shipments to allow you to test the products in your market before committing to full volumes.

What documents do you provide for customs clearance in Armenia?

We provide a full commercial document package for each shipment, including a commercial invoice, packing list, veterinary health certificate issued by Russian authorities, and a certificate of origin. Our export team coordinates documentation to ensure smooth customs clearance on the Armenian side.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Yerevan typically take?

Transit time by refrigerated road freight from Kazan to Yerevan is generally 5 to 9 business days depending on the route, border crossing conditions, and your chosen logistics provider. We confirm lead times for each order at the time of booking and keep you updated throughout transit.

Do you offer Private Label (white label) production for Armenian distributors?

Yes. We offer Private Label manufacturing for distributors and retail chains who wish to sell our products under their own brand. Minimum monthly volumes for Private Label programs start at 500 kg per SKU. We handle packaging design adaptation, labeling in required languages, and product customization within our production capabilities. Contact our export team to discuss your requirements.
