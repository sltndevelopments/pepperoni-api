Halal pepperoni & sausages wholesale from the manufacturer — expand full description

Kazan Delicacies is a halal meat products manufacturer for HoReCa and retail based in Kazan, Russia. Since 2022 we produce pepperoni, sausages, hot dogs, meat semi-finished products and traditional Tatar pastries wholesale. All products are certified Halal (Muslim Spiritual Board of Tatarstan No. 614A/2024), compliant with HACCP and ISO 22000:2018. Our in-house laboratory controls quality at every stage — from incoming raw materials to shipment of finished products in corrugated boxes.

Pepperoni Wholesale

Halal pepperoni is our flagship product. We produce boiled-smoked and dry-cured pepperoni from beef, horse meat and turkey. Diameter from 22 to 55 mm, any cut: sliced, diced, whole stick. Gas-flushed or vacuum packaging. We supply pepperoni wholesale for pizzerias, bakeries, delivery services and retail chains. Contract slicing to customer specifications. Consistent batch-to-batch quality with laboratory confirmation and test protocols.

Sausages & Frankfurters Wholesale

We produce boiled, boiled-smoked and dry-cured sausages wholesale from halal raw materials: beef, chicken, turkey, horse meat. Our range includes hot dogs, frankfurters, cooked hams, burger patties, meat toppings and meat trimmings for pizza. All sausages are pork-free, GMO-free, sodium nitrite-free — clean ingredients for your consumer. For retail chains we offer gas-flushed packaging with extended shelf life and transport packaging in 5–10 kg corrugated boxes.

Tatar Pastries Wholesale

Traditional Tatar pastries made from authentic recipes: gubadiya with kort, belish with meat, echpochmak, kystyby, triangles, vak-belish. Deep shock freezing preserves taste and texture. We supply pastries wholesale for cafes, culinary shops, canteens, retail outlets and catering. Halal ingredients, premium flour, beef and chicken fillings. Minimum order — from 1 box of any product.

Supply Terms & Private Label (OEM)

We operate across Russia and export to CIS countries and the UAE. EXW Kazan shipment or delivery via our distribution warehouse in Lyubertsy (Moscow region). We accept orders from 1 box of any catalog item. Individual terms for distributors, wholesale buyers and retail chains. Private Label (OEM) — we produce sausages and pastries under your brand: from recipe and specification development to serial production in your packaging. Full-cycle contract manufacturing of halal products at our own factory in Kazan.

Kazan Delicacies products are already available in retail chains across Tatarstan and neighboring regions. We export to Kazakhstan, Belarus, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Azerbaijan and the UAE. For export shipments we provide a complete package of documents: veterinary certificates, Halal certificates, TR CU declarations. Contact our sales department for the current price list and delivery cost calculation to your city.
