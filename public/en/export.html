Countries of halal certificate recognition and veterinary export accreditation

HALAL · EAEU · Gulf states

Kazan Delicacies exports halal meat products. Export opportunities depend on two factors: recognition of the halal certificate in the importing country and veterinary accreditation of the facility for supplies (the "Cerberus" registry of Rosselkhoznadzor). Below are the current country lists for both directions.

1. Countries recognizing the halal certificate

Products are certified by the Halal Standards Committee of DUM RT (certificate #614A/2024). The body holds international SMIIC and GSO/GCC accreditation, as well as JAKIM (Malaysia) and EIAC (UAE) accreditation. As a result, the certificate is recognized in the following countries:

2. Veterinary accreditation for export

The Kazan Delicacies facility (No. RU-016/SB88273) is listed in the "Cerberus" registry of supervised facilities of Rosselkhoznadzor. Accredited export destinations:

🇰🇿 Kazakhstan 🇰🇬 Kyrgyzstan 🇧🇾 Belarus 🇦🇲 Armenia 🇹🇯 Tajikistan 🇬🇪 Georgia 🇦🇿 Azerbaijan 🇺🇿 Uzbekistan
