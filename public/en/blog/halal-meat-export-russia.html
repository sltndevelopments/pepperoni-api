Halal Meat Export from Russia: Multi-Country Distribution & Currency Flexibility

March 27, 2026 | Kazan Delicacies

Kazan Delicacies has secured Customs Union certification, enabling streamlined halal meat exports to six countries across the Eurasian region. With pricing available in seven currencies and flexible EXW Kazan delivery terms, the company offers B2B buyers and distributors a comprehensive solution for sourcing premium halal protein products from Russia.

Export Coverage at a Glance

  • Countries: Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Belarus, Armenia, Azerbaijan
  • Currencies supported: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN
  • Delivery terms: EXW Kazan (Incoterms 2020)
  • Certification: Halal #614A/2024 MSB Tatarstan, Customs Union compliant
  • Price lists: Available in Excel format with language, currency, and VAT options

Customs Union Certification: Opening Six Markets

The acquisition of Customs Union certification represents a significant milestone for Kazan Delicacies' export operations. This credential streamlines cross-border trade by ensuring compliance with unified technical regulations across member states, eliminating redundant certification requirements in each destination market.

For international buyers, this translates to simplified procurement procedures, reduced administrative overhead, and faster delivery timelines. The certification covers Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Belarus, Armenia, Uzbekistan, and Azerbaijan—markets with combined Muslim populations exceeding 50 million consumers and growing demand for certified halal meat products.

Seven-Currency Pricing System

Currency volatility remains a persistent challenge in international food trade. Kazan Delicacies addresses this concern by offering price quotations in seven currencies: Russian Ruble (RUB), US Dollar (USD), Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT), Uzbek Som (UZS), Kyrgyz Som (KGS), Belarusian Ruble (BYN), and Azerbaijani Manat (AZN).

Buyers can download current price lists directly from the company's API portal at api.pepperoni.tatar, selecting their preferred language, currency, and VAT calculation method. This automated system ensures pricing transparency and eliminates confusion caused by exchange rate fluctuations between order inquiry and contract finalization.

EXW Kazan: Clear Delivery Terms

All export shipments operate under EXW (Ex Works) Kazan terms according to Incoterms 2020 standards. Under this arrangement, buyers assume responsibility for transportation, export clearance, and logistics from the Kazan production facility.

This delivery structure offers maximum flexibility for established distributors with existing logistics networks while maintaining price clarity. Buyers retain full control over carrier selection, routing decisions, and customs brokerage arrangements—critical factors for businesses operating across diverse regulatory environments.

For buyers without established freight forwarding partnerships, Kazan Delicacies can facilitate introductions to vetted logistics providers experienced in temperature-controlled meat transport across Eurasian corridors.

Domestic Distribution Network

Beyond international exports, Kazan Delicacies maintains an active domestic distribution network across the Russian Federation. Weekly shipments serve Moscow, with regular deliveries to St. Petersburg, Ufa, Yekaterinburg, Samara, Saratov, Volgograd, Dagestan, and Chechnya.

Within Tatarstan, the company operates its own delivery fleet, ensuring direct service to regional customers. This dual-market approach provides production stability and enables competitive pricing through economies of scale.

Product Range and Halal Certification

All products carry halal certification #614A/2024 issued by the Muftiate Spiritual Board of Tatarstan, one of Russia's most recognized Islamic authorities. The certification covers the complete production chain from slaughter protocols through processing, packaging, and storage.

The product portfolio includes a comprehensive range of beef, lamb, and poultry items suitable for HoReCa operations, retail distribution, and food manufacturing applications. Detailed specifications, packaging configurations, and minimum order quantities are available in the downloadable price lists.
