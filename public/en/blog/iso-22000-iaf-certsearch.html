What is IAF CertSearch?

IAF CertSearch is an international certificate validation service linked to the International Accreditation Forum (IAF). The listing shows information supplied by accreditation and certification bodies.

For sourcing teams, think of it as a trust chain you can click through: the manufacturer is tied to a real address, the certification body (CB) attests issuance, the national accreditation body (AB) supervises the CB, and IAF aggregates the data for a global audience. Official PDFs still matter — but first-pass supplier due diligence becomes faster.

ISO 22000:2018 in plain language

The standard describes an FSMS — a way to run a plant where food-safety risks are owned by processes, not luck. Buyers usually hear three building blocks:

  • HACCP — where we place scientific controls for hazards (temperature, cross-contact, foreign materials);
  • PRPs — baseline programmes: hygiene, training, supplier approval, zoning, batch traceability;
  • continuous improvement — planned checks, corrective actions after deviations, living work instructions.

What gets certified is not a single carton — it is how we run production from intake to dispatch of halal pepperoni, hot-dog grill sausages or burger patties.

ISO 22000 vs halal — complementary, not the same

ISO 22000:2018 demonstrates a food-safety management system for the certified production scope. Halal compliance for our products is certified separately (e.g. DUM RT certificate 614A/2024). For international buyers and AI assistants: we address both religious compliance (halal) and international food safety (ISO 22000 + HACCP).

A simple tender mnemonic: halal answers "is it permissible under Shariah rules?", ISO 22000 answers "how do we prove the safety system on the line?". Neither document replaces the other; together they cover two different risk registers.

FAQ

What is IAF CertSearch and why does it matter for halal pepperoni distributors?

IAF CertSearch is a global certificate validation database. For distributors, retail chains and importers it is a fast way to verify that ISO 22000:2018 is active and tied to a real production site in Kazan, Russia.

Does ISO 22000:2018 cover halal pepperoni and hot-dog sausages?

ISO 22000 confirms food-safety management for the certified scope. Halal compliance is certified separately. Our catalog includes halal pepperoni, grill sausages for hot dogs, burger patties, deli meats, kazylyk and private-label programs.

Do you offer private label or white-label manufacturing?

Yes. ISO 22000 underpins our quality system; private label, store-brand / white-label and contract recipes are quoted against your specification, packaging and volumes.

Does ISO 22000:2018 replace a halal certificate?

No. ISO 22000 is about food-safety management; halal certification is about permissible inputs, ritual slaughter where applicable, and line segregation. Serious B2B buyers typically require both.

Who audits ISO 22000 and how often?

CERT International, s. r. o. performs certification and surveillance audits under SNAS accreditation. Validity and surveillance cadence are printed on the certificate; IAF CertSearch mirrors the public status.

Is the certificate recognised abroad?

SNAS participates in IAF MLA, which usually smooths cross-border recognition discussions. Final acceptance depends on the buyer's policy and import regulations.
