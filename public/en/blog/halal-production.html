Halal Sausage Production Features

February 2026 · Kazan Delicacies

Halal is not just a label, but a strict production standard. In Kazan Delicacies, every stage is controlled by the Halal Standard Committee of DUM RT (certificate No. 614A/2024).

What Makes Our Products Special

  • Raw materials — only beef, horse meat, chicken and turkey. No pork is used.
  • Slaughter — according to Halal rules, with complete bleeding of the carcass.
  • Naturalness — minimum additives: salt, black pepper, natural spices. No GMOs, no transglutaminase (meat glue).
  • Control — HACCP system at every production stage.
  • Production — made in Tatarstan, on the territory of the Agro-Industrial Park Kazan.

Certificates

Halal DUM RT No. 614A/2024 — a real production audit, not self-declaration. HACCP — traceability of each batch from raw materials to consumer.
