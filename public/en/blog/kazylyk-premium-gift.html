Kazylyk Premium: Gift Packaging and Slicing for Restaurants

Kazylyk Premium in gift packaging is a solution for restaurants, hotels, and corporate clients who value halal product quality and presentable service. Professional 2-3 mm slicing, vacuum packaging, and certified production preserve taste for 90 days when stored at 0 to +6°C. This format is ideal for meat platters, banquet menus, and gift sets for restaurant guests.

Premium Packaging Standards for HoReCa

The hospitality industry has strict requirements for product quality and appearance. Kazylyk premium segment must meet standards that ensure not only impeccable taste but also marketable appearance throughout shelf life.

Vacuum packaging is a key technology for preserving delicacy freshness. It removes oxygen, preventing fat oxidation and microorganism growth. For HoReCa restaurants, this means stable product quality from receipt to guest service.

Requirements for Packaging Materials

  • Multi-layer polymer films with barrier properties — protection from oxygen and moisture penetration
  • Material transparency — visual quality inspection without opening packaging
  • Tear resistance — maintaining seal integrity during transport and storage
  • Food safety — materials approved for contact with meat products
  • Print capability — production date, expiration date, barcode for tracking

Gift packaging for the restaurant segment often includes an additional cardboard box or wooden base. This enhances presentation and protects the product from mechanical damage during delivery to customers.
