  • Quality System: HACCP-certified facility with full traceability

  • Production Location: Kazan Agro-Industrial Park, Tatarstan, Russia

Understanding Halal Certification Standards

Halal certification represents a rigorous production standard that extends far beyond ingredient selection. At Kazan Delicacies, our MSB Tatarstan certificate #614A/2024 reflects genuine third-party auditing and continuous oversight—not self-declaration or unverified claims.

The Muslim Spiritual Board of Tatarstan (DUM RT/MSB Tatarstan) conducts comprehensive facility inspections covering supplier verification, slaughter procedures, cross-contamination prevention, ingredient compliance, and production line protocols. This certification provides B2B buyers with documented assurance that products meet authentic Islamic dietary requirements recognized across Muslim-majority markets.

Raw Material Selection and Sourcing Controls

Halal meat production begins with strict protein selection. Our manufacturing exclusively processes beef, horse meat, chicken, and turkey—all sourced from certified halal suppliers who adhere to Islamic slaughter requirements. Pork and pork-derived ingredients are completely excluded from our facility, eliminating any cross-contamination risk.

Each animal undergoes slaughter according to zabiha principles, including proper invocation, swift severance of major vessels, and complete blood drainage. These requirements aren't merely religious formalities—they directly impact meat quality, shelf life, and flavor characteristics that discerning buyers expect.

Supplier documentation is maintained for full traceability, with halal certificates verified for every raw material batch entering our production facility.

Clean Label Manufacturing Approach

Modern consumers—whether in retail, foodservice, or institutional settings—increasingly demand transparency and minimal processing. Our halal sausage production aligns with these clean label preferences through deliberate ingredient restriction.

Formulations utilize only essential components: premium meat cuts, salt, black pepper, and natural spice blends. We explicitly exclude:

  • GMO ingredients
  • Transglutaminase (meat glue)
  • Artificial colorants
  • Synthetic flavor enhancers
  • Non-halal additives or processing aids

This minimalist approach delivers authentic taste profiles while meeting both halal requirements and contemporary clean label expectations—a significant advantage for HoReCa operators building menu transparency and retail buyers responding to health-conscious consumers.

HACCP Integration and Quality Assurance

Halal certification operates alongside our HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points) quality management system, creating dual-layer product safety and compliance assurance.

Every production batch undergoes documented monitoring at critical control points including receiving inspection, thermal processing verification, metal detection, and final packaging integrity checks. This systematic approach provides complete traceability from incoming raw materials through finished product distribution.

For B2B buyers managing their own food safety programs, this documentation integration simplifies supplier verification, regulatory compliance, and internal quality audits. Each shipment includes comprehensive batch records supporting your downstream traceability requirements.
