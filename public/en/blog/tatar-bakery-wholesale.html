Signature Tatar Bakery Products

Our traditional Tatar bakery line preserves authentic recipes passed down through generations, adapted for industrial-scale production without compromising quality or taste. Each product is carefully crafted to meet both traditional standards and modern food safety requirements.

Gubadiya with Kort – A ceremonial multi-layered pie featuring kort, a distinctive Tatar cottage cheese mixture. This festive pastry traditionally graces celebration tables and represents the pinnacle of Tatar baking craftsmanship. Available in 100g portions.

Echpochmak – The iconic triangular pastry filled with beef and potatoes. The name translates to "three corners" in Tatar, referencing its distinctive shape. This savory pastry is a staple in Tatar households and food service establishments throughout the region. 100g portions.

Peremyach – Traditional fried meat pie with a tender, fluffy texture. Typically served warm, this product offers excellent versatility for HoReCa operators seeking authentic ethnic menu items. 100g portions.

Samsa – Available in two variants: chicken and beef. These Central Asian-influenced pastries feature flaky layers and seasoned meat fillings, offering familiar yet distinctive menu options for diverse customer bases.

Elesh – A substantial pie combining chicken and potatoes in a golden pastry shell. At 150g per piece, this product serves well as a main course option or hearty snack offering.

Chak-chak – The crown jewel of Tatar confectionery. This honey-soaked pastry symbolizes Tatar hospitality and celebration. Deep-fried dough pieces bound with honey create a unique texture and appearance that makes an impressive dessert presentation. Available in 300g packages.
