February 2026 · Kazan Delicacies

Kazan Delicacies received a Customs Union conformity certificate, opening access to the markets of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Belarus, Armenia, Uzbekistan and Azerbaijan.

Delivery Terms

Supplies are made on EXW Kazan terms (Incoterms 2020). Prices are available in 7 currencies: RUB, USD, KZT, UZS, KGS, BYN, AZN.

The current price list can be downloaded in Excel format directly from the main page api.pepperoni.tatar — with language, currency and VAT selection.
