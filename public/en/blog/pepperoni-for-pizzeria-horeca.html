The Challenge: Pepperoni That Stays Flat

Professional kitchen operators know the problem well: pepperoni slices that curl up during baking, creating an uneven appearance and inconsistent eating experience. This curling occurs when fat renders unevenly or when the meat-to-fat ratio isn't optimized for high-temperature cooking.

Kazan Delicacies has engineered halal pepperoni specifically for food service applications. Our heat-stable formulation ensures slices maintain their circular shape (50-55 mm diameter) at temperatures up to 350°C. This thermal stability means consistent presentation across every pizza, whether in a conveyor oven, deck oven, or wood-fired setting.

The product is available in both cooked-smoked and dry-cured variants, offering flexibility for different menu applications and regional taste preferences. Each format delivers the characteristic pepperoni flavor profile while meeting halal requirements.

Product Range and Packaging Options

Understanding that different operations have different needs, we offer multiple configurations:

Cooked-Smoked Classic — Our flagship product combining chicken and beef, available in 0.5 kg pre-sliced packs for immediate use or 1 kg whole chubs for operations that prefer in-house portioning.

Cooked-Smoked Horse Meat — A regional specialty popular in Central Asian markets, available in 0.5 kg sliced format. Horse meat offers a distinctive flavor profile and is traditional in Tatar cuisine.

Dry-Cured Pepperoni — For operators seeking a more intense, traditional pepperoni flavor with lower moisture content. Available in both 0.5 kg sliced and 1 kg whole formats.

All products are vacuum-sealed to ensure maximum shelf life and food safety during distribution. Our HACCP-certified facility in Kazan, Tatarstan ensures consistent quality control at every production stage.

Why Halal Certification Matters for HoReCa

Halal certification (MSB Tatarstan #614A/2024) isn't just about religious compliance—it's a quality indicator that signals rigorous oversight of sourcing, handling, and processing. For food service operators, halal certification opens doors to underserved market segments and demonstrates commitment to inclusive menu offerings.

Muslim consumers represent a significant and growing dining demographic. Pizzerias and restaurants with verified halal options can capture market share in regions with Muslim populations while also appealing to consumers who associate halal with higher quality and ethical sourcing standards.

Our certification covers the complete supply chain, from livestock sourcing through final packaging, providing the documentation needed for your menu labeling and marketing materials.
