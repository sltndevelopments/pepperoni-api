Halal Pirozhki Wholesale for Cafeterias and Cafes

Pirozhki are a versatile product for any foodservice establishment. From a simple snack to a full meal — the right filling and serving format allow integration into any menu concept. Halal certification expands the audience and guarantees product quality. We offer wholesale supply of frozen pirozhki with various fillings for cafes, cafeterias, canteens, and fast food establishments.

Assortment of Fillings

The range includes pirozhki with beef, chicken, liver, meat-and-potato, meat-and-rice, cabbage, and potato-mushroom fillings. All products are halal certified. The dough is prepared with milk and butter, making it soft and fluffy even after reheating. Each pirozhki weighs 80-100 g.

Supply Terms

Pirozhki are supplied frozen at -18°C. Shelf life is 6 months. Products are packaged in bulk boxes of 3-5 kg or in individual packaging for retail. For catering, baked pirozhki are supplied that only require 3-5 minutes of reheating in the oven at 180°C. Minimum order is 5 kg per filling type, or from 10 kg for mixed assortment.
