Kazan Delicacies Receives ISO 22000:2018 Certification

March 26, 2026 · Company News
Kazan Delicacies has successfully passed certification under ISO 22000:2018 — the international standard for food safety management systems. The certificate was issued by an accredited international certification body and remains valid until 2029.

What ISO 22000 Means for Partners

ISO 22000 is a globally recognized standard that confirms a food production facility has implemented a comprehensive food safety management system. For wholesale buyers and HoReCa operators, it means:

  • Systematic food safety controls are embedded at every production stage
  • A fully transparent and traceable production chain is in place
  • Strict requirements for raw materials, storage, and processing are met
  • Risks are minimized across the entire supply chain — from incoming ingredients to outgoing shipments

In practical terms, this translates to predictable product quality, supply reliability, and documented safety assurance for every batch of halal pepperoni, sausages, and meat semi-finished products we deliver.

Why ISO 22000 Matters for Business

In today's food industry, ISO 22000 is not a marketing badge — it is an operational prerequisite. Without it:

  • Access to large federal distributors and retail chains is restricted
  • Export operations within the EAEU and beyond are more complex
  • Participation in tenders and government procurement is limited

Achieving this certification confirms that Kazan Delicacies is ready to operate under international standards and opens new market opportunities for our partners.

Scope of certification: cooked and cooked-smoked sausage products, meat delicacies, semi-finished products, and frozen goods — the full assortment for HoReCa and retail channels.

Halal Compliance and ISO 22000 — a Unified Quality System

The ISO 22000 framework is integrated with our internal halal standards, creating a dual-layer quality control system at every stage:

  • Verification of raw material origin and halal status
  • Prevention of cross-contamination across production lines
  • Strict sanitation protocols and regular internal audits
  • Full traceability from farm to packaging

Together with the Halal Certificate of the Spiritual Board of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan (No. 614A/2024), the ISO 22000:2018 certification gives our partners maximum confidence in product safety and religious compliance.

What's Next

The ISO 22000 certification is part of a systematic quality development roadmap at Kazan Delicacies. Following our earlier HACCP certification and halal accreditation, the next milestones include expanding export operations and scaling private label production for international partners.
