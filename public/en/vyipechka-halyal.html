Frequently Asked Questions

What is gubadiya?
Gubadiya is a traditional Tatar multi-layer pie with rice, kort (cottage cheese), egg and raisins. Served at celebrations and festivities.
How is peremyach different from cheburek?
Peremyach is a Tatar fried pie with meat filling and a hole on top. Cheburek is a large fried semi-circular pie. Both are halal.
Can I order pastries wholesale for a cafe?
Yes, wholesale supply from 20 kg. Individual piece and tray packaging available for HoReCa. Email info@kazandelikates.tatar.
Do you have pastries with meat filling?
Yes: cheburek, peremyach, echpochmak — with beef meat. All fillings are halal, no pork.
