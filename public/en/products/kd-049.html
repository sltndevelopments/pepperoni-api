в/к Рамазан — Premium Smoked Chicken Deli for HoReCa & Wholesale

в/к Рамазан is a high-end smoked chicken product from the «Копченые» category, crafted for professional kitchens and wholesale distribution. For HoReCa buyers, this product reduces prep time while delivering authentic smoked notes that elevate menu items without additional smoking equipment. Wholesale clients benefit from a reliable, high-turnover SKU that appeals to diverse customer bases, from deli counters to catering operations.

Storage & Logistics: Maintaining в/к Рамазан Quality

в/к Рамазан requires refrigerated storage at temperatures between 0°C and +4°C to preserve its smoked character and safety. Shelf life is 30 days from production date when stored properly in original vacuum packaging. For logistics, use insulated containers with gel packs for short-haul deliveries; long-distance shipments require reefer trucks with temperature monitoring. Once opened, the product should be consumed within 3–5 days or frozen at -18°C for extended storage (up to 3 months). Thawing should occur gradually in refrigeration to maintain texture. Distributors should rotate stock using FIFO system to minimize waste and ensure freshness for end customers.
