Storage & Logistics for Ветчина из Курицы в батоне

Storage requires consistent refrigeration at 0°C to 4°C throughout the cold chain. The product is not suitable for freezing, as thawing compromises emulsion stability and increases purge loss. Vacuum-packaged logs have a shelf life of 90 days from production date when stored unopened; once opened, use within 5 days under proper refrigeration. For logistics, Kazan Delicacies ships via temperature-controlled reefer containers or refrigerated trucks, with temperature data loggers included for all international shipments. Buyers should ensure receiving facilities maintain cold storage at ≤4°C, as temperature abuse accelerates syneresis and color fading. The product’s dense, log format stacks efficiently in standard Euro pallets (80 x 120 cm), with 24 logs per layer and 8 layers per pallet, maximizing container utilization. For airfreight, smaller consignments are packed in insulated EPS boxes with gel packs, suitable for 48-hour transit windows. Customs documentation includes health certificates, halal certificates, and country-of-origin declarations, all pre-cleared for EU and GCC markets.
