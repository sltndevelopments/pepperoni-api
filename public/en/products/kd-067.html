Storage and Logistics for «Чак-Чак в крафтовой подарочной упаковке»

This product requires ambient storage conditions: 15-25°C, away from direct sunlight and strong odors. Unopened boxes maintain peak freshness for 90 days from production date. Once opened, consume within 14 days if stored in an airtight container at room temperature — refrigeration is not recommended as it hardens the honey coating. For logistics, each kraft box is wrapped in a protective polybag to prevent moisture absorption. Master cases contain 12 individual boxes (dimensions: 40x30x20 cm, weight ~5.2 kg). We ship via temperature-controlled freight for long-haul routes (e.g., to Middle East or Asia) to prevent honey crystallization. For European distribution, standard courier services suffice with a 5-7 day transit time. We offer consolidated shipping for mixed pallets combining this item with other «Национальная татарская выпечка» products. Storage tips for HoReCa: keep in dry pantry away from spices; for retail displays, use open shelving with moderate humidity. In case of returns due to damage, we require photographic evidence within 48 hours of delivery for replacement.
