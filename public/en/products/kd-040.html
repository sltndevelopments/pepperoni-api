Storage, Shelf Life, and Logistics Guidelines

«Ветчина из Курицы» requires consistent cold chain management to maintain quality. Store at 0°C to +4°C throughout distribution. The product has a shelf life of 45 days from production date when stored properly, with vacuum packaging extending freshness. For frozen storage, maintain at -18°C for up to 6 months, though we recommend fresh distribution for optimal texture. Thawing should occur gradually in refrigeration to prevent moisture loss. Our logistics team coordinates with third-party cold storage providers to ensure temperature integrity during transit. We recommend FIFO (first-in, first-out) rotation in your warehouse. Cases are stackable and labeled with production date, batch number, and halal certification mark. For international shipments, we provide insulated packaging and temperature monitors upon request. Returns are accepted only for verified cold chain breaches or manufacturing defects.
