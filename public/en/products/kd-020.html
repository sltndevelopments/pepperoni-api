Storage and Logistics for «Филе куриное варное»

Proper storage of «Филе куриное варное» ensures maximum shelf life and quality. The product must be kept frozen at -18°C or below, with a shelf life of 12 months from production date under consistent cold chain conditions. Upon thawing (refrigerator at 0–4°C), it remains viable for 48 hours; do not refreeze after thawing. For logistics, Kazan Delicacies ships via refrigerated trucks with temperature monitoring, covering regions across Russia, Kazakhstan, and select EU markets. For private-label clients, we offer custom pallet configuration to optimize warehouse space. Always store away from raw meats to maintain halal segregation.
