Kazylyk Premium

Traditional air-dried horse meat sausage of Tatar and Bashkir cuisine

HALAL #614A/2024 HACCP

Kazylyk is a traditional delicacy of the Turkic peoples and one of the most iconic symbols of Tatar and Bashkir culinary heritage. This air-dried sausage is crafted from premium horse meat using time-honored recipes and natural spices. For centuries, kazylyk has been considered an honored treat, served at festive tables and offered to esteemed guests.

Our Kazylyk Premium is produced from carefully selected top-grade horse meat. The meat undergoes a lengthy curing process, resulting in a rich, deep flavor and distinctive aroma. The product is fully ready to eat.
