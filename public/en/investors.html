Certified & Export-Ready

Compliance built for halal markets from day one.

Production already runs under halal discipline as a non-negotiable standard — not an adaptation for this proposal.

Halal Certification

Issued by the Spiritual Administration of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan (#614A/2024). Recognized across GCC states (GSO/GCC), plus Kazakhstan, Belarus, Kyrgyzstan, Malaysia (JAKIM), Jordan, South Africa, Thailand, and others — full recognition map.

Active

ISO 22000:2018

Food Safety Management System (CERT International, SNAS-accredited, certificate FSMS-2351/А). Public verification: IAF CertSearch.

Active

HACCP

Hazard analysis and critical control points implemented across the facility — the operational layer alongside ISO 22000:2018, covering raw-material intake through finished-goods release.

Active

Veterinary Export Accreditation

Facility RU-016/SB88273 attested in Rosselkhoznadzor Cerberus for export to Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Belarus, Armenia, Tajikistan, Georgia, Azerbaijan, and Uzbekistan. Gulf destinations (UAE, KSA, etc.) — veterinary terms confirmed per contract.

Attested

EAEU declaration of conformity (TR CU 021/2011) applies to all product lines. Full certification package, batch records, and audit history available in the data room on request.
