FAQ – Cape Town import & pastry

How long does sea freight take to Cape Town?

Mor freight from Russia to Durban port takes 30–40 days, then ~2 days to Cape Town. We manage full container logistics and all documentation.

Do you have SANHA or other South African halal certification?

Our halal certificate is issued by DUM RT (#614A/2024), widely accepted by SANHA. We also provide Russian veterinary certificates for each batch.

What is the minimum order quantity for Cape Town?

For HoReCa clients we start from 200 kg mixed pallet (frozen). Full container (20 ft) holds ~12 tons. LCL possible for trial.

Do you offer private label for pastry?

Yes, Private Label available. We pack under your brand with your design. MOQ from 500 kg per SKU.

What is the shelf life and storage conditions?

Frozen pastry: 12 months at -18°C. Ready products: 72 hours at +2..+6°C. No sodium nitrite, clean label.
