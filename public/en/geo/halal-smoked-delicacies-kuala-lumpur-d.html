From Kazan with smoke – Pepperoni Tatar

We started in the old Tatar quarter of Kazan, where meat smoking is a 400‑year tradition. Our founder, Rustam, built the first cold‑smokehouse in 2018 to prove that halal beef and chicken can be free of nitrite yet rich in flavour. Today we supply 120+ Russian restaurants and export to UAE, Singapore and now Kuala Lumpur.

Every piece of smoked brisket, chicken breast and kolbasa is hand‑rubbed with sea salt, then cold‑smoked for 18 hours over beech. No liquid smoke, no accelerators. That’s the Pepperoni Tatar difference.

FAQ – smoked halal meats for Kuala Lumpur buyers

❶ Is the halal certificate accepted by JAKIM?

Yes. Our halal certificate from DUM RT (#614A/2024) is recognised by JAKIM through the international halal mutual recognition framework. We provide a full certification package for import clearance.

❷ Do you ship directly to Kuala Lumpur warehouses?

Absolutely. Sea freight via Novorossiysk – Port Klang (30–40 days) or air freight (7–10 days). We handle all cold chain logistics to your KL facility.

For first orders we work with 500 kg per SKU. Retail, HoReCa and gift box clients can combine products. Private label from 1000 kg.
