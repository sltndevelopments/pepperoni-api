Frequently Asked Questions — Jeddah & Saudi Market

Does the product meet Saudi halal import requirements?

Our products carry Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 issued by a recognized Muslim religious authority. All documentation is prepared in accordance with Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) import requirements, including an Arabic-language halal declaration available on request.

What is the minimum order quantity for a shipment to Jeddah?

The standard minimum for sea freight is one 20-ft refrigerated container — approximately 18–20 tonnes of mixed SKUs. For a first pilot order via air freight, we can discuss smaller volumes starting from 300–500 kg to let your culinary team evaluate the product before committing to a full container.

Can we order custom portion sizes for our restaurant chain's kitchen standards?

Yes. We work directly with executive chefs and procurement managers to agree on portion weight (75 g, 100 g, or 150 g), filling composition, and packaging format before production. This is standard practice for our HoReCa partners — not a premium add-on. Minimum custom run is 500 kg per SKU.

Is Private Label production available for Saudi retail or foodservice brands?

Fully available. We produce пирожки халяль оптом and samsa under client brands with custom packaging, Arabic-language labeling, and nutritional declarations formatted for Saudi market requirements. Design files and regulatory compliance support are included in the Private Label service.

What quality control documentation is provided with each shipment?

Each shipment is accompanied by: halal certificate (DUM RT), HACCP conformity declaration, ISO 22000:2018 audit summary, Russian veterinary certificate (Rosselkhoznadzor), TR CU 021/2011 declaration, batch-specific laboratory analysis report, and a certificate of origin. All documents are available in Russian and English; Arabic translations available for key certificates.
