The Pepperoni Tatar Story — Why Kazan?

Founded in 2022 under the Pepperoni Tatar brand, Kazan Delicacies was built with a single thesis: the global halal meat market deserved an industrial-grade manufacturer that could match the quality standards of conventional European processors — without a single gram of pork, without nitrites, and with full traceability from farm to fork. Kazan, the capital of Tatarstan and the historic center of Islam in Russia, was the natural home for this project. The city's deep-rooted halal food culture, access to high-quality Russian beef and poultry, and proximity to export corridors toward the Gulf and Africa made it the right address.

Today, Kazan Delicacies operates 77 halal SKUs, exports to 10+ countries, and prices in 7 currencies — including USD and AED for African and Gulf buyers. Clients include major Russian retail chains, fast-food operators, and private-label partners. For Khartoum buyers, this means a proven supply chain, not a startup experiment.
