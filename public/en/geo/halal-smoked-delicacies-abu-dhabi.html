Frequently Asked Questions — Abu Dhabi & UAE

Answers to the questions we receive most often from UAE buyers and distributors.

Does your halal certification meet UAE import requirements?
Our halal certificate is issued by DUM RT (Spiritual Board of Muslims of Tatarstan), No. #614A/2024 — a recognised authority accepted by UAE food regulators. All products also carry Russian veterinary certificates (Rosselkhoznadzor) required for each export consignment to the UAE.
What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale supply to Abu Dhabi?
The minimum wholesale order for export starts from 200 kg per SKU. For HoReCa trial orders we offer flexible conditions — a smaller introductory shipment is possible for new partners. Contact our export manager to discuss your specific volume and product mix.
How are the products delivered to Abu Dhabi, and how is cold chain maintained?
We ship via air freight (Kazan–Dubai, ~5 hours, refrigerated cargo) for urgent or premium orders, and by sea (Novorossiysk–Jebel Ali, 14 days, reefer containers) for large volumes. Cold chain is maintained at 0–4°C throughout. We provide full documentation for UAE customs clearance.
Can you produce under our private label for the Abu Dhabi or wider UAE market?
Yes, Private Label is available. We develop custom packaging with your brand identity, Arabic labelling and the required halal marking. Minimum batch for private label production is 500 kg — a realistic volume for a mid-size Abu Dhabi distributor or retail chain.
Why are your smoked products a strong fit for Abu Dhabi's premium hotel and restaurant segment?
Abu Dhabi's luxury hospitality sector — from Yas Island resorts to downtown fine dining — demands products with verifiable clean labels. Our smoked meats contain no sodium nitrite, no artificial colours and no GMO, certified under ISO 22000:2018. We work directly with executive chefs on portion formats and can supply HoReCa-specific packaging tailored to kitchen workflow.
