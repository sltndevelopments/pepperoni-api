Frequently asked — Makassar & halal pelmeni

How does Russian halal certification work with BPJPH in Indonesia?

Our Halal certificate (DUM RT #614A/2024) is issued by the Russian Muslim Spiritual Board, which is a recognised foreign halal body. We provide a notarised translation and halal assurance system file for BPJPH registration. Many Makassar importers use this route.

What is the real delivery time to Makassar port?

Sea freight from St. Petersburg to Surabaya or Jakarta takes 35–45 days. Then feeder vessel to Makassar: 3–5 days. We ship in 20′ reefer containers, temperature -18°C. Door-to-port incoterms: FOB or CIF Makassar.

Can I get pelmeni with my own brand for Makassar retail?

Absolutely. Private label is our core B2B service. We design packaging in Indonesian, include halal logo, nutritional table, and barcode. MOQ 10 MT per SKU. Lead time for first private label order: 12 weeks.

Do you test for nitrite and artificial additives?

Yes, every batch is lab-tested for sodium nitrite (E250) — result: <1 mg/kg. Our recipe contains only beef, chicken, onion, wheat flour, salt, black pepper. No preservatives, no flavour enhancers. HACCP records available for audit.

What is the minimum order for a trial to Makassar?

We offer a 5 kg sample box sent by air cargo to Makassar (3–4 days). Sample cost is deducted from your first container. For bulk: 1×20′ reefer (approx. 12 MT) is standard. Contact us for proforma invoice.
