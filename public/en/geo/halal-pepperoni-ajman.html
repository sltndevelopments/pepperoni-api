Frequently Asked Questions — Ajman & UAE

Is your pepperoni certified halal for the UAE market?

Yes. Our pepperoni carries Halal Certificate #614A/2024 issued by DUM RT — the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan. The certificate is recognized across GCC markets. All raw materials are 100% halal beef, chicken or horse meat — no pork-derived ingredients whatsoever.

What diameter options are available for Ajman pizzerias?

We produce pepperoni sticks in diameters 40, 50, 60 and 75 mm. The 50 mm format is most popular among Ajman pizzeria chains for standard 12-inch pies, while the 75 mm diameter suits gourmet and artisan pizza concepts. Custom slice thickness is available on request for high-volume accounts.

How is the product delivered from Kazan to Ajman?

Two routes are available: air freight Kazan–Dubai (approx. 5 hours transit) for urgent or sample orders, and sea freight Novorossiysk–Jebel Ali (approx. 14 days) for container volumes. We supply EXW Kazan; our logistics partners handle export documentation and UAE customs clearance.

Can we order private-label pepperoni under our own brand?

Absolutely. We manufacture private-label pepperoni to your recipe, slice format and packaging specification — including Arabic-language labelling compliant with UAE food labelling regulations. Contact our export team to discuss minimum order quantities and technical requirements.

What shelf life and storage conditions should Ajman distributors expect?

Vacuum-packed pepperoni sticks have a shelf life of up to 60 days at 0–6 °C, and up to 6 months in frozen storage at −18 °C. Professional export packaging is designed for refrigerated container or reefer air freight, maintaining cold chain integrity from Kazan to UAE warehouses and restaurant kitchens.
