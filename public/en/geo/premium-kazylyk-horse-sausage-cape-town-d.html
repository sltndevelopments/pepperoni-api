Absolutely. DUM RT Halal is recognised by most South African importers. We provide full slaughter certificates and ingredient declarations. Many clients in SA already use our documentation for SANHA acceptance.

Wholesale minimum is 50 kg (mixed SKUs). For full container (20 ft) we offer better incoterms. We can consolidate with other halal meats from our factory.

Sea freight via Durban port — 30–40 days transit. We handle all veterinary, halal, and phytosanitary documentation. Door-to-door DDP possible for regular clients.

Zero sodium nitrite. No artificial preservatives, no GMO, no added colours. Only horse meat, beef fat, natural spices, and sea salt. Naturally air-dried.
