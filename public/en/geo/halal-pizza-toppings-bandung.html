FAQ — Bandung & Indonesia

Our DUM RT certificate #614A/2024 is issued by a recognized Muslim authority and is accompanied by a full export documentation set — including veterinary certificates and ISO 22000 records. We work with our Indonesian import partners to prepare the BPJPH registration dossier. Contact us for the current document package.

What is the minimum order quantity for Bandung delivery?

Export orders start from one refrigerated 20-foot FCL, which we can consolidate across multiple SKUs. EXW Kazan pricing applies; sea freight to Jakarta or Surabaya port runs 35–45 days. For smaller test orders, discuss consolidation options with our export team.

Why won't your pepperoni dry out in Bandung's high-humidity kitchen environment?

Our toppings are formulated with a controlled fat-to-lean ratio and sliced to calibrated uniform thickness on industrial slicers. This means consistent moisture retention during baking — regardless of whether your kitchen runs high ambient humidity or a dry conveyor oven cycle. No curling, no charring, no shrinkage surprises.

Can you produce private-label toppings with Bahasa Indonesia labeling for our brand?

Yes. Our Private Label / SBM service covers recipe customization, slice format, packaging artwork, and multilingual labeling including Bahasa Indonesia. We already manufacture private-label pepperoni for major Russian retail and HoReCa clients. Lead time from approved specification: 4–6 weeks.

Our SKU range covers the full spectrum: thin-crust Neapolitan concepts using sliced pepperoni, American-style thick-crust menus using beef bacon and ham, and street-food pizza-by-the-slice operations using pre-portioned sausage slices.
