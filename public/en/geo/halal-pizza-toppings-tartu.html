FAQ — Tartu & Estonia

Answers to the questions Tartu buyers ask most often

Do you deliver halal pizza toppings to Tartu, Estonia?

Yes. We ship by refrigerated truck from Kazan — approximately 2,100 km, 48 hours transit. We can deliver to your Tartu address directly or to a Tallinn consolidation warehouse. Minimum order and freight cost are confirmed at the enquiry stage.

Is the DUM RT halal certificate recognised for the Estonian and EU market?

DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan) is a recognised Islamic authority with international standing. Our certificate #614A/2024 is accepted by halal-conscious buyers across the GCC, CIS and Baltic region. We provide the full certificate PDF for your procurement compliance file.

Why won't your pepperoni burn or dry out in a pizza oven?

Thermo-stability is a designed-in specification, not an accident. We calibrate slice thickness and fat-to-protein ratio specifically for deck and conveyor ovens at 280–350 °C. The result: slices stay moist, hold their circular shape, and develop an even colour — which matters enormously for delivery-platform photography and customer repeat rates in Tartu's competitive food market.

Can you produce halal pizza toppings under our own restaurant or chain brand?

Yes — private label (СТМ / SBM) is a core part of our business model. We have produced private-label pepperoni for major Russian clients including OMPK. For Tartu buyers, we can adapt recipe, slice format, packaging language (Estonian / English / Russian) and labelling to your specification.

What payment currencies and trade terms do you offer for Estonian buyers?

We invoice in EUR and work with 7 currencies in total. Standard trade term is EXW Kazan; we can assist with freight coordination to Tartu or Tallinn. Export documentation, including veterinary certificates and EUR.1 movement certificate, is provided as standard.
