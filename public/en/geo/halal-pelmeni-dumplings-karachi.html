Production Technology

Why We Don't Need Sodium Nitrite

Most frozen dumpling manufacturers rely on sodium nitrite as a preservative and color stabilizer. At Kazan Delicacies, we made a different engineering choice: rapid blast-freezing at −35°C within minutes of production. This locks in color, texture and microbiological safety without any chemical additives.

The filling is prepared from fresh halal beef and chicken trimmings, seasoned with natural spices, wrapped in a thin dough shell, and immediately frozen on a continuous IQF line. No nitrites. No artificial colors. No compromises on the halal status of the final product.

For Karachi's food professionals — whether running a restaurant chain, a dark kitchen, or a retail culinary counter — this means a product you can serve with confidence and market as genuinely clean-label.
