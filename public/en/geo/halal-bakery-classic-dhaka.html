Frequently asked questions — Dhaka market

❓ Minimum order for Dhaka?

FCL 20 ft (12 pallets, ~8–9 tons). We also offer LCL consolidation with other halal frozen items. Sea freight to Chittagong 25–35 days, then truck to Dhaka.

❓ Halal certificate accepted in Bangladesh?

Yes — DUM RT #614A/2024 is recognised by the Bangladesh Halal Authority. We provide a certificate of analysis and origin.

❓ Shelf life and storage?

12 months at -18°C. After thawing (in fridge) 72 hours. Perfect for high-volume kitchens in Gulshan, Banani, and Old Dhaka.

❓ Can you adapt recipes for Bangladeshi taste?

We adjust spice levels, fillings (chicken, beef, vegetable), and dough thickness. Private label & co-packing for local brands.

❓ Customs & cold chain in Dhaka?

We provide full veterinary docs, TR CU 021/2011, and work with experienced freight forwarders. Delivery to your cold store in Tejgaon or Mohakhali.
