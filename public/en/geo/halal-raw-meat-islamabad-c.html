🚚 Delivery to Islamabad – How It Works

We ship from Russia via sea freight to Karachi port (25-35 days transit). From Karachi, your container is trucked to Islamabad (2-3 days). We provide full documentation: veterinary certificates, halal certification, packing list, invoice. Minimum order: 1x20ft container (approx. 20 tons). For smaller quantities, we work with local distributors in Islamabad. Contact us for current pricing and lead time.
