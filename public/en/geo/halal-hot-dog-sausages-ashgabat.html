Yes. Halal certificate #614A/2024 from DUM RT is recognised across CIS and Central Asian markets. With 93% of Ashgabat's population being Muslim, this certification is the primary compliance requirement — and ours is issued by one of Russia's most authoritative halal bodies.

How long does rail freight from Kazan to Ashgabat take?

Transit by rail typically takes 5–7 days. We ship EXW Kazan, and our export team prepares the full documentation set to minimise delays at the Turkmenistan border. Refrigerated container options are available for temperature-sensitive shipments.

What packaging formats are suitable for fast food and street food operations?

We offer vacuum-sealed retail-style trays for smaller fast food outlets and bulk master-cartons for high-volume operators and distributors. Packaging dimensions, weight per unit and shelf-life labelling can be adjusted to your kitchen's specific storage and rotation requirements.

Do the sausages contain any artificial preservatives or coloring agents?

No. Our hot dog sausages are manufactured on a clean-label formulation: no sodium nitrite, no GMO ingredients, no artificial colors. This is increasingly demanded by Ashgabat's premium HoReCa segment and makes for a strong on-menu quality claim.

Can we order private-label hot dog sausages under our own brand for the Turkmenistan market?
