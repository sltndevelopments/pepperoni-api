Frequently Asked Questions — Medan Buyers

Is your halal certification accepted for import into Indonesia under BPJPH rules?

Our Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 is issued by an internationally recognised Islamic authority. We actively support BPJPH mutual recognition procedures and supply the full halal audit dossier, ingredient declarations, and slaughter process documentation required by Indonesian authorities. Our export team has direct experience navigating this process.

What minimum order quantity makes sense for a Medan HoReCa distributor?

We work with B2B buyers from 200 kg per SKU. For first-time Medan partners, we recommend a mixed trial pallet combining smoked beef, smoked chicken, and sausage lines — this allows your hotel and restaurant clients to evaluate the full range before committing to larger volumes. We discuss MOQ flexibility individually.

How does the product survive a 40-day sea voyage to Sumatra?

Our smoked products are vacuum-packed immediately after production and shipped in refrigerated reefer containers maintaining 0–4 °C. Shelf life from production date is up to 60 days, engineered specifically to accommodate the transit time to Indonesian ports with adequate remaining shelf life for Medan distribution. Each batch has a production date and full cold-chain log.

Can you produce Private Label for our Indonesian brand?

Yes — Private Label is a core part of our export offer. We design packaging with Bahasa Indonesia labelling, your logo and brand identity, and all mandatory Indonesian food labelling fields. Minimum Private Label run is 500 kg per SKU. Lead time from artwork approval to dispatch is 3–4 weeks.

Why is "no sodium nitrite" a selling point specifically in Medan's market?

Medan's premium restaurant and hotel segment — from the Jalan Imam Bonjol corridor to the Deli River waterfront dining scene — is increasingly serving health-aware, label-reading consumers. Operators who can list "no preservatives" on their menus command higher prices and stronger guest loyalty. Our nitrite-free smoked range is one of very few internationally certified options available to Medan buyers at this quality level.
