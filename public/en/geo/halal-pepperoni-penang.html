Frequently Asked Questions — Penang & Malaysia

Is your halal certificate compatible with JAKIM requirements in Malaysia?

Our pepperoni carries DUM RT Halal certificate #614A/2024, issued by the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan — a recognised Islamic authority. While JAKIM is Malaysia's sovereign halal body, DUM RT certification meets the core halal slaughter and processing criteria. We supply full documentation for your local importer and JAKIM assessment. Many GCC and CIS importers have successfully cleared our products through similar review processes.

Which pepperoni diameter is most popular with Penang pizzeria operators?

Based on our HoReCa export experience, 50 mm and 60 mm diameters are the most versatile for standard 10–12 inch pizza formats common in Malaysian fast-casual dining. The 40 mm variant suits mini-pizza and canapé applications, while 75 mm is preferred by hotel banquet kitchens for large-format production. We recommend requesting a mixed sample pack to test with your kitchen team.

MOQ for export shipments is discussed individually based on product mix and logistics format. Sea freight orders typically start from one full pallet; air freight allows smaller trial orders. Can your pepperoni be used in hotel and airline catering supply chains in Penang?

Yes. Our ISO 22000:2018 certification and full traceability documentation meet the supplier qualification requirements of international hotel groups and airline catering operators. Clean-label composition (no nitrites, no artificial colors) is increasingly required by premium HoReCa buyers in Malaysia. We can provide full technical data sheets and allergen declarations on request.

How is the pepperoni packaged for the Malaysian cold chain?

Products are shipped frozen (−18 °C) in vacuum-sealed export packs with English-language labels including ingredient list, net weight, production date, expiry date and halal marking. Shelf life is 180 days from production. Packaging formats (pre-sliced portions, bulk logs, retail-ready packs) are customisable for Private Label orders.
