Frequently asked by Bahrain buyers

1. How do you guarantee no nitrite in every batch?
We use a proprietary fermentation process with celery extract and natural spices. Every production lot is tested by an independent lab (HPLC method) – nitrite content < 1 mg/kg. Certificate of analysis included with each shipment.
2. Is the halal certificate accepted by Bahrain’s municipal authorities?
Yes. DUM RT is one of the recognized halal bodies for meat imports into Bahrain. We also provide a letter of halal compliance from the Russian Muftiate. Our documentation has been cleared multiple times at Khalifa bin Salman port.
3. What is the minimum order for a restaurant in Manama?
For first order: 300 kg (mixed SKUs). Reorders: from 200 kg. We offer consolidated sea freight every 3 weeks. For hotels and chains we can arrange air freight (5 days) for emergency fills.
4. Do you provide shelf‑life and storage conditions for hot climate?
Our sausages have a shelf life of 120 days (vacuum, 0–4°C). For Manama we recommend refrigerated storage at +2..+6°C. We supply with temperature data loggers inside each pallet. Frozen option available for longer storage.
5. Can you adapt recipes for Bahraini taste (more spice, less fat)?
Absolutely. Our R&D team works directly with your chef. We adjust spice blends (black pepper, cardamom, chili), fat content (12–25%), and casing types. Sample batch ready in 7 working days after brief.
