Frequently Asked Questions – Kano Market

How long does shipping take to Kano?
We ship via морфрахт to Lagos port (30–40 days). From Lagos, inland freight to Kano takes additional 3–5 days. We coordinate with your logistics partner.
What is the minimum order quantity for pepperoni?
For first orders, minimum 500 kg (mixed diameters). For Private Label, minimum 1000 kg. We can discuss smaller trial batches for Kano restaurants.
Are your halal certificates accepted in Nigeria?
Yes — DUM RT is recognized by major halal bodies in northern Nigeria. We also provide additional documentation for SON (Standards Organisation of Nigeria) if needed.
Do you offer samples for Kano chefs?
Absolutely. We send 1 kg sample packs (beef, chicken, horse) with full spec sheets. Chefs in Kano can test texture, taste, and cooking performance before ordering.
Can you produce pepperoni with my brand label?
Yes — Private Label is our specialty. You provide design, we produce and pack. Minimum 1000 kg per SKU. We ship to Kano under your brand.
