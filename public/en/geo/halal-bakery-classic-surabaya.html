FAQ: Halal Baked Goods Supply to Surabaya

Indonesia's BPJPH requires that imported halal products carry certification from a recognized foreign halal body. Our DUM RT #614A/2024 certificate is the formal basis for this process, and we provide full documentation to support your local importer's registration. We recommend working with an accredited Indonesian halal consultant to finalize mutual recognition — our export team will assist throughout.

Standard sea freight from Russian ports to Surabaya (Tanjung Perak port) takes 35–45 days via transhipment in Singapore or Port Klang. We ship exclusively in reefer (refrigerated) containers maintained at -18°C. Our frozen baked goods have a 180-day shelf life, so even after transit you receive full commercial shelf life at destination.

We offer frozen semi-finished (bake-off) and fully cooked ready-to-heat formats. Portion weights range from 80g to 200g per piece, packed in 5 kg or 10 kg professional trays. For high-volume fast-food operators in Surabaya, we can configure custom portion weights and packaging formats starting from 1 FCL production run.
