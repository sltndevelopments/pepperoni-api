❓ Frequently asked — Mombasa buyers

1. How long does sea freight take to Mombasa?

Direct морфрахт from Novorossiysk to Mombasa takes 28–35 days. We use reefer containers with constant -18°C. Chilled mince (0–2°C) can be shipped via air freight on request (7 days).

2. Do you provide halal certificates accepted by Kenyan authorities?

Yes — DUM RT halal certificate #614A/2024 is recognized by KEBS and Kenya Port Health. We also attach Russian veterinary certificates and HACCP/ISO docs for smooth clearance.

3. Can I get a sample shipment before bulk order?

Absolutely. We offer trial pallets (500 kg) for Mombasa buyers — chilled or frozen. Contact us for lead time and pricing. Samples can be sent with DHL (3 kg).

4. What is the minimum order quantity for minced meat?

MOQ is 1 full pallet (approx. 800 kg) for mixed SKUs. For private label we require 5 pallets. We can combine beef, chicken, and turkey in one container.

5. Do you have distribution partners in Mombasa?

We work with a bonded warehouse in Mombasa port (cold storage). We can arrange last-mile delivery to your kitchen or retail point. Also partner with a local halal distributor in Nyali.
