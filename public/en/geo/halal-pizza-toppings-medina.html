The Pepperoni Tatar Story

Kazan Delicacies was founded in 2022 in Kazan — capital of the Republic of Tatarstan and the historic Muslim centre of Russia. The Pepperoni Tatar brand was built from the ground up for one purpose: to give the global halal food industry a reliable, industrial-grade source of pizza toppings that require no compromise on religious compliance or culinary performance.

Medina represents one of the most demanding food markets in the world. With over 1.3 million residents, 6,000+ restaurants and a hospitality infrastructure that scales dramatically during Hajj and Umrah seasons, local pizzerias and hotel chains face acute pressure on supply consistency. Kazan Delicacies was designed precisely for this: stable volumes, documented cold chain, and toppings engineered for professional ovens — not home kitchens.

FAQ — Halal Pizza Toppings for Medina

Are your pizza toppings certified halal for import into Saudi Arabia?

Yes. All products carry Halal certificate #614A/2024 issued by DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan), plus HACCP, ISO 22000:2018 and TR CU 021/2011. Russian veterinary certificates are issued per shipment. 100% beef and poultry — absolutely no pork, no lard, no forbidden additives.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Medina take, and can you handle Hajj-season demand spikes?

Standard sea freight via Novorossiysk–Jeddah takes approximately 12 days, with onward refrigerated transport to Medina in 4–6 hours. For Hajj and Umrah seasons, we recommend advance planning with buffer stock. We work with clients on seasonal forecasting to ensure uninterrupted supply during peak hospitality periods.

What pizza topping formats are available for pizzerias and hotel kitchens in Medina?

We supply sliced pepperoni (Ø 25–45 mm, 1.5–3 mm thickness), beef bacon strips, halal mini sausages and sliced cooked beef ham — all calibrated by industrial slicer for consistent portioning. Custom slice dimensions and formats are available under Private Label agreements.

Can you produce toppings under our restaurant chain or distribution brand (private label)?

Yes. We offer full Private Label / SBM manufacturing: custom recipe development, seasoning profile, slice format and branded packaging. We already produce the Aslam pepperoni line for OMPK (Ostankino Meat Processing Plant). MOQ and lead times are agreed individually — contact us to discuss your specification.

What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale supply to Medina, and in which currency do you invoice?

MOQ depends on the specific SKU and delivery terms. We invoice in USD, SAR, AED or EUR — whichever suits your procurement process. Contact us at info@kazandelikates.tatar for a personalised commercial offer, product samples and a full certificate package within 24 hours.
