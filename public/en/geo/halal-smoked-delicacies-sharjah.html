Natural Smoking: The Technology Behind the Taste

Most industrial smoked meats on the GCC market rely on sodium nitrite (E250) for colour fixation and extended shelf life. We deliberately chose a different path: traditional chamber smoking over hardwood, combined with precise temperature control and vacuum sealing.

The result is a product with a genuine smoke ring, authentic aroma and a shelf life of 30–90 days at 0–6°C — without a single synthetic preservative. For Sharjah chefs who read ingredient labels and for retail buyers facing increasingly informed Muslim consumers, this is not a minor detail. It is the entire value proposition.
