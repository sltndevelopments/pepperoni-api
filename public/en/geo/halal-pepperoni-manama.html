FAQ — Halal Pepperoni Wholesale for Manama

Is your halal certificate valid for import into Bahrain?

Our certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 is issued by the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan — one of Russia's oldest and most authoritative halal bodies. We provide Arabic-language halal declarations alongside Russian veterinary certificates, which together satisfy Bahrain's National Health Regulatory Authority (NHRA) import requirements. We recommend that importers verify current NHRA acceptance criteria with their customs broker, and we are happy to supply any additional documentation requested.

How is the pepperoni shipped to Manama and what is the transit time?

We ship in refrigerated containers (−18 °C) from Novorossiysk. The sea route to Khalifa Bin Salman Port, Bahrain takes approximately 15 days. Frozen pepperoni has a 12-month shelf life from production date, so the transit time represents less than 5% of total shelf life — leaving Bahraini distributors and operators with ample stock rotation time.

Can Manama hotel chains and large HoReCa groups order under their own brand?

Yes. Private Label manufacturing is a core service. We produce to your recipe specification, apply your brand artwork to packaging, and can adapt spice intensity to Gulf consumer preferences (milder or bolder profiles). MOQ for private-label export is one pallet (~500 kg). Lead time from recipe approval to first shipment is typically 3–4 weeks.

What pepperoni formats work best for Manama's fast-casual and delivery pizza segment?

For high-volume delivery pizzerias, we recommend pre-sliced 50 mm or 55 mm beef pepperoni in 1 kg vacuum packs — consistent slice count, minimal prep time, and portion-cost control. For premium dine-in concepts, 65–75 mm slices in 500 g trays create a visual impact on open-kitchen pizzas. Our team can send samples before you commit to a full order.

Do you offer pricing in AED or USD for Bahraini buyers?

Yes. We quote in 7 currencies including USD and AED. Prices are EXW Kazan; we can also arrange CFR Manama pricing through our freight partners. Volume discounts apply from 3 pallets. Contact us at info@kazandelikates.tatar for a current price list.
