100 kg per SKU. For a full container (20 ft) we recommend 12–14 tons. We consolidate with other products if needed.

4. Can you develop a recipe specific for Omani taste?

Absolutely. We formulate pepperoni, sausages, and cutlets with local spice profiles. Clean label, no nitrite, and we adapt fat/meat ratios.

5. Do you offer deferred payment for distributors in Maskat?

Yes, for established partners with bank references we offer 30–60 day deferred payment. First orders are prepaid or L/C.
