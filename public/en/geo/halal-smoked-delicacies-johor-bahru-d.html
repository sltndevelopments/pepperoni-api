FAQ – Johor Bahru & halal smoked meats

1. Do your certificates satisfy JAKIM import requirements?

Yes. DUM RT halal (#614A/2024) is accepted by JAKIM for beef, chicken and processed meats. We provide full halal chain documentation with each shipment.

2. What is the typical delivery time to Johor Bahru?

Sea freight (Novorossiysk – Port Klang) takes 30–40 days, then ~1 day to JB. Air freight 7–10 days door-to-door. We use reefer containers or temperature-controlled air cargo.

3. Can you supply smoked chicken for hotel buffet in JB?

Absolutely. Our smoked chicken breast and whole smoked chicken are popular in 4-5 star hotels. Natural smoke, no nitrite, vacuum packed. Custom portion sizes available.

4. Do you offer private label for halal gift sets?

Yes. We produce premium gift boxes with your branding: smoked beef, chicken sausage, duck breast. Minimum 200 kg per SKU. Halal certified packaging.

5. How do you ensure halal integrity during shipping?

Every container is sealed and accompanied by halal certificate & veterinary cert. No mixing with non-halal goods. Cold chain monitored 24/7.
