Frequently asked — Sabah HoReCa

Is Pepperoni Tatar certified by JAKIM?

Our Halal DUM RT certificate (#614A/2024) is accepted by JAKIM under the mutual recognition framework. We provide a halal guarantee letter and full slaughterhouse traceability for your JAKIM audit.

What’s the minimum order for a pizza chain in KK?

For sea freight we ship 20-ft containers (≈12 pallets, ~5.4 tons). For air freight we can start from 500 kg. We also combine with other halal Russian meats to lower MOQ.

How do you maintain cold chain to Kota Kinabalu?

Reefer containers set at 0–2°C, with real-time temperature loggers. From Port Klang we use cold trucks to KK. Air freight uses active gel packs and insulated boxes.
