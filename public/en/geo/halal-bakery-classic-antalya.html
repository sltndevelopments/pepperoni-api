Frequently Asked Questions — Antalya Market

Answers to the questions we hear most often from Turkish HoReCa buyers and distributors.

Is your halal certificate accepted by Turkish authorities and Antalya hotel procurement teams?
Yes. Our Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024, issued by the Spiritual Board of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan, is recognised by Turkish import authorities and by the procurement standards of major international hotel groups operating on the Antalya Riviera. We provide a certified translation upon request.
How does the cold chain work on the Novorossiysk–Samsun–Antalya route?
Products leave our Kazan facility at −18 °C in sealed refrigerated trucks. The Novorossiysk–Samsun ferry leg takes approximately 36 hours with continuous refrigeration. From Samsun, a direct truck run reaches Antalya in under 12 hours. Total door-to-door transit is 2–4 days depending on ferry schedules and Turkish customs clearance speed.
What minimum order quantity applies for restaurants and cafes ordering halal bakery products wholesale in Antalya?
The minimum B2B order starts from 100 kg per SKU. Mixed pallets — for example, combining halal pies wholesale, samsa and chebureki — are available from a total of 300 kg. We can discuss flexible terms for anchor buyers in the Antalya region who commit to monthly volumes.
Can Antalya-based food distributors launch their own Private Label halal bakery line?
Absolutely. We have experience supporting distributors in export markets who want to build a local halal brand. Minimum production run starts from 500 kg per SKU. We coordinate packaging design, provide all certification documentation in your brand name, and can produce in English-language packaging for the Turkish market.
How do chefs in Antalya kitchens work with your frozen bakery products?
Our frozen pies, samsa and chebureki are supplied ready-to-bake or ready-to-fry — no additional preparation or marinating required. Baking time is 12–18 minutes from frozen in a standard convection oven. We provide technical sheets and recommended cooking parameters for each SKU, and our team is available for remote consultation with your head chef.
