Frequently Asked Questions — Mombasa & Kenya

Answers specific to importing halal pizza toppings into Kenya

Do you supply halal pizza toppings to Mombasa, Kenya?

Yes. We export to East Africa via sea freight through Mombasa Port — transit time is 28–35 days EXW Kazan. We prepare all Russian export documentation, veterinary certificates, and halal paperwork required for Kenyan customs clearance. Your local importer or freight forwarder handles Kenyan import duties and KEBS registration.

Is the halal certificate recognised in Kenya?

Our halal certificate is issued by DUM RT — the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan, one of Russia's principal Islamic authorities. Kenyan halal importers and hotel procurement teams operating under Supreme Council of Kenya Muslims (SUPKEM) standards have accepted this certification. We recommend confirming with your local compliance officer for specific chain requirements.

What is the minimum order for a container shipment to Mombasa?

The practical minimum for sea freight is one refrigerated 20-foot container (approximately 18–20 tonnes net). We can consolidate multiple SKUs — pepperoni, beef bacon, halal ham, sausages — in a single container to help you reach MOQ while diversifying your topping range from day one.

How do the toppings perform in Mombasa's coastal kitchen conditions?

Coastal humidity affects product handling, not the toppings themselves — they are vacuum-packed and kept frozen until portioning. Our thermo-stable formulation was specifically developed for high-heat ovens (wood-fired, conveyor, deck), which are common in Mombasa's pizza restaurants. Chefs report no drying or burning issues at standard pizza baking temperatures.

Can we get private-label pizza toppings for our Kenyan restaurant brand?

Absolutely. We produce private-label (SBM) halal pizza toppings with your brand name, custom spice profile, slice thickness, and packaging design. This is popular with Kenyan pizza chains and dark kitchen operators who want a proprietary topping identity. Contact us to discuss volumes and lead times.
