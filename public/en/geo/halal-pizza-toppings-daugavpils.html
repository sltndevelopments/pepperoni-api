Frequently Asked Questions — Daugavpils Buyers

How do you deliver halal pizza toppings to Daugavpils?

We ship by refrigerated road freight from Kazan to Riga (1,900 km, approximately 42 hours). From Riga, onward delivery to Daugavpils is arranged via our Latvian logistics partners. Orders are consolidated weekly. Minimum order from 50 kg per SKU; full pallet orders receive priority scheduling.

Our pizzeria serves a mixed audience — can we use your toppings on a non-halal menu?

Absolutely. Our toppings are 100% halal by default, but there is no requirement to market your menu as halal-only. Many Daugavpils operators use our pepperoni and beef bacon simply because of clean-label quality and consistent slicing — the halal certification is a bonus that expands your potential customer base.

What shelf life and storage conditions do the toppings require?

Chilled (+2 to +6 °C): up to 45 days in unopened vacuum or MAP packaging. Frozen (−18 °C): up to 9 months. We recommend chilled supply for active pizzeria use; frozen bulk is suitable for distributors and central kitchens. All packaging is labeled in Russian and English with storage instructions.

Can I get samples before placing a wholesale order?

Yes. We send a paid sample kit (3–5 SKUs, approximately 2–3 kg total) via express courier to any address in Latvia. The cost of samples is credited against your first B2B order. Contact us at info@kazandelikates.tatar to arrange.

Do your products comply with Latvian / EU import requirements for meat?

Each shipment is accompanied by Russian veterinary certificates (Rosselkhoznadzor) and TR CU 021/2011 compliance documentation. We work with experienced customs brokers for Latvia and the Baltic states. Our export team will advise on current import regulations specific to your product category before the first order.
