Why does thermostable slicing matter specifically for Doha pizzerias?

Most Doha pizzerias run deck or conveyor ovens at 280–350 °C to meet high throughput demand. Standard meat toppings dry out, curl or char at those temperatures, which increases food waste and degrades presentation. Our thermostable slicing process retains moisture and natural colour through the full bake cycle — a measurable advantage for any kitchen running 200+ pizzas per shift.

Can you produce pizza toppings under our own brand for the Qatari or GCC market?

Yes. We offer full Private Label / SBM manufacturing: custom recipe, slice thickness, casing type, seasoning, and packaging design including Arabic labelling. Minimum order is one refrigerated FCL (20-foot container). We have existing private-label experience with GCC distributors and with OMPK's «Aslam» brand in Russia.
