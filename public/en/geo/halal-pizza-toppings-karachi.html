FAQ — Halal Pizza Toppings for Karachi

Is your halal certification accepted for import into Pakistan?

Our certificate is issued by DUM RT — the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan, an established halal authority with recognition across GCC, CIS and Asian markets. We supply full halal documentation with each shipment. We recommend confirming acceptance with your Pakistani customs broker, as PSQCA may require additional endorsement for specific product categories.

How does sea freight to Karachi work, and what is the lead time?

We ship EXW Kazan. Products are consolidated at a Russian export hub and loaded into a reefer container for sea freight to Karachi Port — transit time is 25–35 days. We provide full cold-chain documentation: veterinary certificates, certificate of origin, halal certificate, packing list and invoice in the format required by Pakistani customs.

What is the minimum order for a first shipment to Karachi?

The practical minimum for a reefer sea-freight shipment is one 20-ft container. We work with Pakistani food importers, distributors and large HoReCa chains. For smaller trial orders we can discuss groupage (LCL) options — contact us to evaluate your specific volume and product mix.

Can Karachi pizzeria chains get a custom private-label topping under their own brand?

Yes — this is one of our core B2B services. We develop the recipe, slice format and packaging to your specification and produce under your brand name. English and Urdu labelling is available. Several clients in the GCC and CIS already supply their HoReCa networks with Kazan Delicacies-manufactured private-label toppings.

Why do your toppings perform better in high-temperature pizza ovens than standard imported products?

The key is our controlled fat-to-protein ratio combined with calibrated slicer cutting. Each slice is uniform in thickness, which means it heats evenly in deck, conveyor or wood-fired ovens reaching 280–350 °C. The result: no moisture loss, no curling, no dark edges — and a consistent topping appearance that matters in Karachi's competitive QSR and premium pizza segment.
