Frequently asked questions — Cape Town
1. Do your products have SANHA certification?

Our halal certificate (DUM RT #614A/2024) is accepted by SANHA and major Muslim bodies in SA. We also provide a halal letter for each container.

2. What is the minimum order for Cape Town?

Wholesale min. 500 kg (mixed positions: pies, chebureks, samsa). For first order we recommend 1 ton to optimize logistics.

3. How do you ship to Cape Town? Delivery time?

морфрахт to Durban (30–40 days), then refrigerated truck to Cape Town (+2–3 days). Full temperature control -18°C.

4. Can I get a sample before ordering?

Yes, we can send frozen samples via courier. Contact our export department.

5. Do you offer deferred payment for distributors?

For established partners with credit history we offer 30–60 day deferred payment. Subject to agreement.

🚢 Delivery to Cape Town – logistics

морфрахт (sea freight): from Novorossiysk / Saint Petersburg to Durban – 30–40 days transit. Then refrigerated truck to Cape Town (1,600 km, ~2 days). We handle all documentation: phytosanitary, veterinary, halal certificates, customs clearance.

Cold chain: -18°C throughout. Products arrive frozen, ready for your freezer. We ship in 20' reefers, max 26 pallets.

*For urgent orders we can arrange air freight (samples only).
