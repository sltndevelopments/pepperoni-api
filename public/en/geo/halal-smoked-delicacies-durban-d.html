Durban – specific questions

Does your halal certification work with SANHA requirements?

Yes. DUM RT #614A/2024 is internationally recognised and accepted by SANHA for import. We can provide a letter of halal equivalence upon request.

What is the minimum order for a restaurant group in Umhlanga?

For HoReCa we start at 200 kg per product. Mixed pallets available. Delivery to your cold store in Durban or direct to your kitchen.

Can you adapt smoking profiles for South African taste?

Absolutely. We offer three smoke intensity levels: mild (beech), medium (alder+oak), and robust (hickory). Our R&D team in Kazan can adjust recipes for peri-peri or BBQ notes.

Do you have a distributor in Durban or do we import directly?

Currently we work directly with importers, retail chains, and HORECA groups. We handle all export documentation, clearing agents in Durban can be recommended.

What is the shelf life of your smoked sausages without nitrite?

Vacuum-packed: 120 days at 0–4°C. Once opened, 7 days. Our natural smoking and low-water activity ensure safety and flavour stability.
