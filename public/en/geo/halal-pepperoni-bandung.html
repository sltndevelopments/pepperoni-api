Our pepperoni carries the DUM RT halal certificate #614A/2024, issued by the Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan. We provide full documentation for Indonesian customs and BPJPH compliance review, including an English-language halal certificate and ingredient declaration.
How long does sea freight from Kazan to Bandung take, and what are the storage conditions on arrival?
Transit time is 35–45 days via refrigerated sea freight EXW Kazan to Jakarta (Tanjung Priok) or Surabaya (Tanjung Perak), then trucked to Bandung. We produce sticks in diameters 40, 45, 55, 65 and 75 mm. Most Bandung pizzerias working with thin-crust or Neapolitan-style bases prefer 40–55 mm for even coverage. American-style and thick-crust operators typically choose 65–75 mm. Our team can advise on the optimal diameter for your specific pizza format.
