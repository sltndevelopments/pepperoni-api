Natural Smoking: The Technology Behind the Taste

Most industrial smoked meats rely on sodium nitrite (E250) to achieve their pink color and extend shelf life — a shortcut that is increasingly rejected by informed buyers in the Gulf region. Our process uses controlled hardwood combustion in traditional smoking chambers: real smoke, real time, real flavor. The result is a product with a naturally brown-amber color, authentic smoky aroma, and a clean ingredient list that any chef can show to their guests with confidence.

For Medina's hotel F&B teams and restaurant kitchens serving pilgrims from over 180 countries, this transparency is not a marketing point — it is a sourcing requirement. Our smoked beef, smoked chicken, and halal smoked sausages are produced in a HACCP-certified facility where every batch is tested before dispatch.
