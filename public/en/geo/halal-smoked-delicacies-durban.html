Is your halal certification recognised alongside SANHA in Durban?

Our Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 is issued by the Spiritual Board of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan, an internationally recognised Islamic authority. How long does sea freight from Russia to Durban take, and how do you handle the cold chain?

Standard reefer container transit from Russia to the Port of Durban is 30–40 days. Products are loaded at +2°C to +4°C and the reefer maintains temperature throughout. The minimum export order is one refrigerated 20-foot container — typically 8–10 tonnes of mixed product lines. You can consolidate smoked beef, chicken and sausage SKUs in a single container, which is the most cost-effective approach for Durban importers and distributors.
