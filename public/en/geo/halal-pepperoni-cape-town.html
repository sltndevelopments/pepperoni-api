Frequently asked questions — Cape Town

1. Does your halal pepperoni have SANHA certification?
We are certified by Halal DUM RT (#614A/2024), which is recognized by SANHA. Our certificates are accepted by major Cape Town importers and halal authorities.

Minimum order is 500 kg per SKU (mixed diameters). We offer consolidated containers to reduce freight costs.

Sea freight via Durban takes 30–40 days. We handle all documentation (veterinary, halal, certificate of origin).

Yes, we send chilled samples by courier. Contact us for a Cape Town tasting session with your chef.
