Frequently asked questions — Lagos importers & HoReCa

How does the 30–40 day sea freight to Lagos affect product quality?

Our pelmeni are flash-frozen at -35°C and shipped in temperature-controlled reefers. We have confirmed cold chain integrity from Kazan to Apapa port. In Lagos we work with bonded cold storage partners to maintain -18°C until final delivery.

Are your halal certificates recognized by Nigerian Islamic authorities?

Yes. Our certificate (DUM RT #614A/2024) is issued under the Russian Muslim Spiritual Board, accepted by major Nigerian halal importers. We also provide additional documentation for NAIC (Nigeria Arabic & Islamic Commission) if required.

Can you supply private label pelmeni for Lagos retail chains?

Absolutely. We offer private label with your brand, custom packaging (1kg, 500g, 250g), and bilingual labeling (English + Hausa/Yoruba). MOQ starts at 10 tonnes per SKU.

What is the minimum order for HoReCa in Lagos?

For restaurants and catering we start at 200 kg mixed pallet (beef/chicken). We consolidate with other frozen halal products to reduce freight cost. Direct delivery to your kitchen in Lekki, Ikeja, or Victoria Island.
