Frequently asked questions — Lagos buyers

How long does sea freight take from Russia to Lagos?
Direct sea freight from Novorossiysk to Lagos port takes 30–40 days. We use reefer containers with full temperature control.
Do you have separate halal production lines for beef, chicken and turkey?
Yes, each protein type is processed on dedicated lines with full segregation. All equipment is cleaned according to halal protocol before production.
Which halal certification body issued your certificate for Nigeria?
Our halal certificate is issued by the Spiritual Board of Muslims of Tatarstan (DUM RT #614A/2024), recognised by major Nigerian importers and halal authorities.
Do you supply private label minced meat for Lagos restaurants?
Absolutely. We offer private label packaging for chains, hotels, and catering companies. Minimum order 5 metric tons per SKU.
What is the shelf life of your chilled halal mince?
Chilled mince (0–4°C) has 14 days shelf life from production. Frozen mince (-18°C) is stable for 24 months. Both options available for Lagos.
