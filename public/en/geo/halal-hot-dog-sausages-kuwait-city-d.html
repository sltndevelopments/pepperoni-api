Yes. Our Halal certificate (DUM RT #614A/2024) is recognized by the Kuwaiti authorities. We provide a full halal chain declaration and slaughterhouse approval.

What is the minimum order quantity for Al-Kuwait?

We work with B2B orders from 1 pallet (approx. 400 kg). For full container loads (20' or 40') we offer volume discounts.

How do you ensure the cold chain during 15 days sea freight?

We use reefer containers with constant temperature monitoring (-18°C). Data loggers are included. Port of Al-Kuwait direct discharge.

Can you produce sausages with Kuwaiti brand packaging?

Absolutely — private label with your logo, bilingual Arabic/English, barcode, and nutritional facts. We already do this for 5+ Gulf brands.

Are the sausages suitable for street food & food trucks?

Yes. Our hot dog sausages have a firm bite, grill well, and are pre-cooked. Perfect for high-volume fast food and street vendors in Kuwait City.
