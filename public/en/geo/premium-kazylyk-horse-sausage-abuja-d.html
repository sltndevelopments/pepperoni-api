Abuja import & halal FAQ

📦 How long does sea freight to Lagos take? What about Abuja delivery?
Direct from St Petersburg to Lagos: 30–40 days. After customs clearance, we use a bonded truck to Abuja (3–5 days). Total door-to-door ~45 days. We provide tracking.
🕌 Is kazylyk halal for Nigerian Sunni/Shafi’i consumers?
Yes. Horse meat is halal (mubah) in Hanafi and Shafi’i schools. Our DUM RT certificate follows the strictest standards: hand-slaughter, Tasmiyah, no stunning. We also provide a letter from Mufti of Tatarstan.
