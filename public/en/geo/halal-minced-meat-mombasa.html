Pepperoni Tatar: From Kazan's Halal Tradition to East Africa's Tables

Pepperoni Tatar was founded in Kazan — the capital of Tatarstan and one of Russia's oldest Islamic cultural centres. Halal is not a marketing label here; it is the founding principle. Every cut, every blend, every batch of фарш халяль оптом (halal mince wholesale) leaves our facility under the supervision of the Spiritual Board of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan (DUM RT), certificate #614A/2024.

Over the years we have built an export infrastructure designed for long sea routes. Mombasa — East Africa's largest port and gateway to a rapidly growing halal consumer market of over 1.2 million residents — is a natural fit for our product range. We work directly with chefs, procurement managers and retail buyers, not through layers of intermediaries, so you get manufacturer prices and full traceability from slaughter to shelf.
