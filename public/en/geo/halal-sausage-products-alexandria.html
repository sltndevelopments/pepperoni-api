Alexandria's food service sector operates under a unique set of pressures: Egyptian consumers are deeply knowledgeable about halal authenticity, hotel chains on the Corniche demand HACCP-backed supply chains, and the street food and fast-casual segment is growing at double digits. What the market has lacked is a consistent wholesale source of sausage products that combine genuine halal certification, internationally recognized food safety standards, and a clean ingredient list — without the sodium nitrite that dominates conventional processed meat products.

Pepperoni Tatar fills that gap directly. Our sausage range — produced in Kazan, Russia under ISO 22000:2018, HACCP, and Halal DUM RT #614A/2024 certification — reaches Alexandria via refrigerated sea freight in 8 days from Novorossiysk. No compromises on cold chain. No hidden additives. No sodium nitrite. Just a product your kitchen team and your customers can trust.

From Kazan to Alexandria — the Pepperoni Tatar story. Founded in Kazan, the capital of Tatarstan and one of Russia's most prominent Muslim-majority regions, Pepperoni Tatar was built on a single conviction: that halal meat products should be as clean and honest as the religious standard they carry. Tatarstan has a centuries-old tradition of halal food culture, and our production team draws on that heritage while operating to the strictest modern food safety frameworks. When we decided to serve the Egyptian market, Alexandria was the natural starting point — a city that understands both tradition and quality.
