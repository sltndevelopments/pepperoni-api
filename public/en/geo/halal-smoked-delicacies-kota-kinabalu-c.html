Frequently asked — Kota Kinabalu import & supply

1. What is the minimum order for smoked halal products to Kota Kinabalu?
Minimum wholesale lot is 500 kg (mixed positions). For first-time partners we offer 300 kg trial. Sea freight FCL or air cargo.
2. Do you have JAKIM recognition or equivalent halal certification?
Our halal certificate is issued by the Muslim Religious Board of Tatarstan (DUM RT #614A/2024). It is accepted by JAKIM through bilateral halal recognition agreements with Russia. We provide full documentation for JAKIM registration.
3. How long does sea freight from Novorossiysk to Port Klang take?
Direct sea freight takes 30–40 days. From Port Klang to Kota Kinabalu we arrange cold-chain trucking + ferry (3–5 days). Air freight via Kuala Lumpur: 7–10 days door-to-door.
