Frequently Asked Questions — Dubai & UAE

Is your halal certification recognized for import into the UAE?

Our Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 is issued by the Spiritual Board of Muslims of the Republic of Tatarstan. We recommend confirming mutual recognition with your UAE importer or Dubai Municipality-approved halal certifier, as the UAE may require an additional endorsement from an ESMA-recognized body for retail positioning. For HoReCa supply, many Dubai buyers accept the DUM RT certificate directly.

How long does delivery from Kazan to Dubai take, and what are the cold-chain conditions?

Air freight Kazan–Dubai takes approximately 5 flight hours, with total door-to-door logistics of 3–5 business days including customs clearance. Products travel at −18°C in insulated containers. Sea freight via Novorossiysk–Jebel Ali takes around 14 days and is cost-effective for orders above 1,000 kg.

What is the minimum order for Dubai HoReCa buyers, and is выпечка для кафе оптом available in small batches?

Minimum order starts from 100 kg per SKU for air shipments and 500 kg per SKU for sea freight consolidation. For first-time Dubai partners, we offer a mixed-SKU trial order to test all three product lines — pies, chebureki, and samsa — before committing to larger volumes.

Can you provide Arabic labeling and Private Label packaging for our Dubai brand?

Yes. We offer full Private Label production with Arabic and English ingredient lists, nutritional tables, and halal certification markings on the packaging. This is particularly popular with Dubai restaurant chains expanding across the GCC who want consistent branded products across all locations.

Do your products contain additives or ingredients restricted under UAE food regulations?

No restricted additives. Our baked goods contain no sodium nitrite, no GMO ingredients, no artificial colors, and no flavor enhancers beyond natural spices. The clean-label formulation complies with TR CU 021/2011 and is fully documented for UAE customs submission.
