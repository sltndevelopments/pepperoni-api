Frequently asked — Johannesburg buyers

1. Is the ham certified halal by SANHA?
Our certificate is from DUM RT (#614A/2024), which is accepted by SANHA and the major mosques in Joburg. We provide a halal letter for each shipment.
2. What packaging do you use for the ham?
Vacuum-packed in 1–5 kg units, suitable for restaurant slicing. Professional polyamide casings. Also available: gas-flushed trays for retail.
3. Do you have a minimum order for a restaurant in Sandton?
Yes, 500 kg per delivery. But we can consolidate with other Joburg buyers. Contact us for a shared container option.
4. How do you ensure no nitrite in the ham?
We replace sodium nitrite with natural fermentation and celery extract. Third-party lab tests confirm no detectable nitrite. Clean label guaranteed.

Absolutely. We welcome Joburg chefs and distributors. Just arrange via info@kazandelikates.tatar. We’ll organize a tour and tasting.
