Why Penang? Why Now?

Penang Is Malaysia's Most Demanding
Halal Food Market

Penang sits at the intersection of Malaysia's two food identities: a UNESCO-recognised street food heritage and a rapidly modernising F&B industry with over 9,000 registered HoReCa outlets. With 60% of the island's 800,000 residents Muslim and the state operating under JAKIM — the world's most rigorous halal certification authority — every product entering a professional kitchen must carry airtight documentation. Half-measures don't survive here.

Our halal pelmeni (Russian-style filled dumplings) are manufactured under a dual-certified regime — HACCP and ISO 22000:2018 — and carry the DUM RT Halal certificate #614A/2024, supported by Russian veterinary certificates and TR CU 021/2011 compliance. For Penang's chefs, purchasing managers and retail buyers, that means one less compliance headache and one more reliable SKU on the menu.
