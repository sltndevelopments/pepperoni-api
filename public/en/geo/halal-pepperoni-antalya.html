Frequently Asked Questions — Antalya & Turkey

Is your halal certificate accepted by Turkish customs and food authorities?

Our halal certificate is issued by DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan), which is a recognized Islamic authority. Turkish importers working with Russian halal products routinely use this certificate for customs clearance and for presenting to retail or HoReCa clients. We recommend confirming acceptance with your customs broker for your specific HS code.

We ship EXW Kazan in refrigerated transport. The ro-ro ferry from Novorossiysk to Samsun runs regularly and maintains cold-chain integrity.
