Production Technology: Why No Sodium Nitrite

Most industrial smoked meats on the European market rely on sodium nitrite (E250) to fix colour and extend shelf life. We deliberately reject this approach. Our facility uses a slow cold-smoke and hot-smoke cycle with alder and cherry wood, which naturally preserves the product through pH reduction and moisture control — without any synthetic preservatives.

The result: a genuinely clean label — no GMO, no artificial colours, no nitrites. For Riga's health-conscious HoReCa operators and retail buyers positioning premium or ethnic ranges, this is a real commercial differentiator. Chefs working with our копчёная говядина оптом note consistent texture and a pronounced natural smoke profile that cannot be replicated with liquid smoke additives.

Every batch is tested under HACCP critical control points and ISO 22000:2018 procedures before dispatch. Traceability from raw material to finished product is documented and available on request.
