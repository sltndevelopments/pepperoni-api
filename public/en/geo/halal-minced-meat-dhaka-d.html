Frequently asked questions — Dhaka market

1. Do you deliver frozen mince to Dhaka restaurants and hotels?
Yes. We ship in 20-ft reefer containers to Chittagong port (25-35 days), then customs clearance and truck delivery to Dhaka. All containers have temperature loggers. We work with local clearing agents.
2. Is your halal certificate accepted by Bangladeshi importers?
Our DUM RT halal certificate (#614A/2024) is recognized by major Bangladeshi buyers. We also provide a halal slaughter affidavit and Russian veterinary certificates. Additional notarization available.
3. Can you produce private label mince for Dhaka retail chains?
Absolutely. We offer private label packaging with your brand design. MOQ 5 tons per SKU. Halal, clean label, no sodium nitrite. We also do custom fat ratios (10% to 20%).
4. What is the minimum order quantity for a restaurant in Dhaka?
For direct container orders: 20 ft (approx 12-15 tons). For LCL (less container) we can consolidate with other buyers — minimum 2 tons. Contact us for details.
5. How do you ensure halal integrity during shipping?
Each container is sealed at our plant in Kazan. Halal certificate covers the whole batch. No mixing with non-halal cargo. We provide photos of seals and temperature records.
