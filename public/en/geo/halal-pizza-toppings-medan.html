From Kazan to Medan: The Pepperoni Tatar Story

Kazan Delicacies was founded in 2022 in Kazan — the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan and the Muslim capital of Russia. The city has been the centre of Islamic culture in the Russian Federation for centuries, which means halal manufacturing here is not a marketing claim but a deeply embedded operational standard. From day one, the factory was designed as a dedicated halal facility: no pork ever enters the premises, and every production batch is traceable from farm to slice.

The Pepperoni Tatar brand grew out of a specific problem: pizzerias across Russia and the CIS were struggling to find halal pepperoni that behaved like real pepperoni in the oven — curling slightly, releasing fat evenly, staying moist. We engineered our beef pepperoni recipe and slicing parameters to match exactly that performance profile. Today we supply OMPK (Ostankino Meat Plant) under the "Aslam" private label, Tatneft petrol station chains, EuroSpar, Bahetle and Metro — and we're bringing the same industrial-grade product to Southeast Asia.
