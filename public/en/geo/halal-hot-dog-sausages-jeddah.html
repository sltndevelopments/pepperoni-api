FAQ — Hot Dog Sausages for Jeddah

Questions from Jeddah HoReCa operators and distributors

Do your hot dog sausages carry a halal certificate accepted in Saudi Arabia?

Yes. Our products are certified halal by DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan), certificate #614A/2024. The facility operates under HACCP and ISO 22000:2018. All shipments include Russian veterinary certificates required for Saudi customs clearance through Jeddah Islamic Port.

What is the lead time for delivery to Jeddah?

Sea freight via Novorossiysk–Jeddah takes approximately 12 days. Air freight Kazan–Riyadh is available in 5 hours for urgent top-up orders. We ship EXW Kazan; your freight forwarder or ours handles all export and Saudi customs documentation. Pricing available in SAR, USD, and AED.

Is Private Label manufacturing available for Saudi brands?

Yes. We produce hot dog sausages under your brand name (SBM / Private Label). Custom recipe, casing diameter, packaging design, and Arabic labeling are all available. This is proven — we already manufacture private-label pepperoni for OMPK / «Aslam» in Russia. Contact us for MOQ details.

Do your sausages contain sodium nitrite or artificial additives?

No. Our hot dog sausages are clean-label: no sodium nitrite, no GMO, no artificial colors. This is especially valued by Jeddah's premium restaurant groups, hotel F&B departments, and health-conscious fast-food operators who need to communicate ingredient transparency to Saudi consumers.

What packaging formats work best for Jeddah commercial kitchens?

We offer vacuum-sealed professional bulk packs in 5 kg and 10 kg export cartons, with MAP (modified atmosphere packaging) options for extended shelf life during sea freight. All packs include cold chain specs, Arabic-language labeling, and full traceability documentation for SFDA compliance.
