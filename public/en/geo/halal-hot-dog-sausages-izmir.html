FAQ: Hot Dog Sausages for İzmir Buyers

Do your hot dog sausages carry a halal certificate valid in Turkey?

Yes. Halal Certificate #614A/2024 is issued by DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan), an authority recognized across OIC-member trade flows. The certificate confirms 100% halal production — no pork, no cross-contamination. Full documentation package available for Turkish customs and retail buyers on request.

How long does delivery from Kazan to İzmir take?

Standard route: Kazan → Novorossiysk (truck) → Samsun (ferry, 2–3 days) → İzmir (truck, 1 day). Total door-to-port transit is approximately 5–8 business days, depending on customs clearance speed. We ship EXW Kazan; we can recommend freight forwarding partners for the full route.

What packaging formats are available for İzmir HoReCa and fast food operators?

We offer professional vacuum packs (1 kg, 2.5 kg, 5 kg) and export master cartons (10 kg). Refrigerated shelf life: up to 30 days. For high-volume street food and fast-food chains, we can configure portion-count packs to match your station workflow. Private-label packaging with Turkish-language labeling is available from agreed MOQ.

Is there sodium nitrite or artificial coloring in the sausages?

No. Our hot dog sausages are clean-label: no sodium nitrite, no artificial colors, no GMO. This matters for İzmir's premium HoReCa segment, where menus increasingly feature ingredient transparency — and for operators targeting health-conscious diners along the Aegean coast.

Can we launch a private-label hot dog sausage brand for the Turkish market?

Yes. Private Label (SBM) is a core service. We adapt recipe, casing diameter, and packaging design to your specifications, including Turkish-language labeling and country-of-origin compliance. We have active private-label partnerships with OMPK / «Aslam» and other B2B clients. Contact us to discuss MOQ and lead times for the Turkish market.
