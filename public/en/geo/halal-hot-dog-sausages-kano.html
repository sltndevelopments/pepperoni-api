Frequently Asked Questions — Kano Buyers

Answers tailored to the northern Nigerian food market

Do your hot dog sausages comply with halal standards accepted in northern Nigeria?

Yes. Our sausages carry Halal certificate DUM RT #614A/2024 issued by the Spiritual Board of Muslims of Tatarstan, covering the full production cycle from raw material to sealed packaging. The certificate is recognised by Muslim communities across West Africa and can be presented to local halal authorities in Kano for verification.

How is delivery from Kazan to Kano organised, and how long does it take?

We ship in refrigerated sea containers (−18 °C) to Lagos port — transit time is 30–40 days. From Lagos, goods are transported by refrigerated truck to Kano. We prepare the full export documentation package including Russian veterinary certificates and halal paperwork, reducing customs clearance time on the Nigerian side.

What is the minimum order quantity for restaurants and fast food chains in Kano?

The minimum export order is 500 kg per SKU. For HoReCa operators we can combine beef and chicken sausage variants within one pallet to reach the MOQ. We also offer trial shipments for new buyers to validate product quality before committing to full container volumes.

Can you produce Private Label sausages for a Nigerian food brand?

Absolutely. We offer end-to-end Private Label: brand name, Arabic halal marking, English ingredient list formatted for NAFDAC registration, and custom packaging sizes. MOQ for Private Label is 1,000 kg. Lead time from artwork approval to dispatch is approximately 3–4 weeks.

Are the sausages suitable for high-volume street food and fast food operations in Kano's climate?

Yes. Our hot dog sausages are vacuum-packed and maintain quality for up to 60 days at −18 °C, making them well-suited for Kano's supply chain realities. The 1 kg and 2.5 kg HoReCa formats are designed for rapid throughput in busy kitchens, and the pre-cooked formulation reduces preparation time for street food vendors operating high-volume grills.
