Why Chittagong, Why Now

Chittagong's food-service market is evolving fast: international hotel brands along the Agrabad corridor, a booming fast-food strip on GEC Circle, and a growing café culture among the port city's young professional class are all driving demand for premium processed proteins that are verifiably halal, clean-label and chef-ready. Production Quality Control

From Raw Material to Reefer Container: How We Control Quality

For a Chittagong importer buying from 6,000 km away, production transparency is everything. Here is the documented process your procurement team can audit.

1
Raw Material Incoming Control

Every beef, turkey and chicken delivery is tested for pH, temperature history and microbiological load before entering the production floor. Supplier certificates are cross-checked against our approved vendor list — no exceptions.

2
Halal Slaughter Verification

Raw materials are sourced exclusively from DUM RT-approved halal slaughterhouses. The halal chain of custody is documented at each handover point, and our QC team conducts periodic unannounced audits at supplier facilities.

3
In-Process HACCP Monitoring

Seven critical control points (CCPs) are monitored in real time during cooking, cooling and packaging. Temperature logs are archived for 3 years and available to buyers on request — important for BSTI import audits in Bangladesh.
