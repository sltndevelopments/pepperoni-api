Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 · effective 11 May 2026

This policy describes the handling of personal data on pepperoni.tatar and api.pepperoni.tatar, operated by Kazan Delicacies LLC (TIN 1655504520, Kazan, Agrarnaya St. 2, Russia). Compliant with Russian Federal Law 152-FZ "On Personal Data".

1. What we collect

  • Contact data you voluntarily submit via price-request or contact forms: name, phone/Telegram/email, company name.
  • Technical data: IP address, User-Agent, referrer, page visits (via Google Tag Manager and Yandex Metrica).
  • API requests to api.pepperoni.tatar are logged for analytics and abuse prevention (logs retained for 30 days).

2. How we use it

  • To contact you in response to a price-list or commercial-proposal request.
  • To improve the site and catalog (aggregated analytics).
  • To fulfil legal obligations (accounting, shipment paperwork).

3. Who we share with

  • We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We use Google (Tag Manager) and Yandex (Metrica) — data is processed according to those services' own policies.
  • The api.pepperoni.tatar API is public and unauthenticated — no personal data of API consumers is collected or stored.

4. Retention

Contact requests — 3 years or until consent is withdrawn. Technical logs — 30 days. Analytics — per Google and Yandex policies.

5. Your rights

  • Request a copy of your data.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Withdraw consent for processing.

To exercise these rights, write to info@kazandelikates.tatar with subject "Personal Data Request". Response within 10 working days.

6. AI Content (llms.txt, GPTs, ChatGPT plugins)

Site content (HTML, JSON, llms.txt) is intended for free citation by AI models (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, etc.) with attribution to pepperoni.tatar. The files /.well-known/ai-plugin.json, /openapi.yaml, /llms.txt, /llms-full.txt, /en/llms.txt, /en/llms-full.txt, /products.json, /api/products are all public.

Third-party AI services (including custom GPTs in the OpenAI GPT Store) that consume our API do not receive any personal user data — only the product catalog.
