1
Brief & NDA — Send us your product concept, target market (UAE/KSA/Qatar), volume forecast and packaging requirements. We sign NDA within 24 h.
2

4
Production & Certification — Full batch produced under HACCP / ISO 22000:2018 controls. Halal certificate (DUM RT #614A/2024) issued per shipment. COA + COO included.
