What Is Private Label / White Label / OEM Bakery?

🏷️ White Label

We manufacture a ready product from our proven recipes. You apply your brand, packaging, and language. Fastest route to market — ideal for distributors and retail chains.

🔧 Private Label

We co-develop the recipe to your specification. Your flavour profile, your ingredients, your exclusive formula — fully protected and produced under contract.

🏭 OEM Contract Manufacturing

Full-cycle production: R&D, sourcing, baking, freezing, packing, labelling, and export documentation. You focus on sales; we handle the factory.
