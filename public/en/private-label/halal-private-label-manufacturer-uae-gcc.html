What Is Halal OEM / White Label Manufacturing?

🏷️ White Label

We produce finished Halal products — you apply your retail label. Fastest route to shelf. Ideal for UAE supermarkets and GCC distributors launching own-brand SKUs.

⚙️ OEM (Custom Recipe)

Your proprietary recipe, your spice blend, your format. We manufacture to your specification with full R&D support, matching your target market's taste profile.

📦 Private Label

Choose from our proven product range, co-brand with your identity. Full packaging design support, Arabic labelling, and UAE S 2055 / GCC Halal compliance documentation.
