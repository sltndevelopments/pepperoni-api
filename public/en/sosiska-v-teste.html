Why the breakfast sausage is made specifically for sausage rolls

The main pain for sausage-roll producers is that an ordinary sausage bursts and releases juice inside the dough when baked, the dough goes soggy and the item loses its appearance and cut. We solved this by design: the breakfast sausage is produced without a casing and calibrated to 45 g. Without a casing it does not split inside the dough, holds its shape, gives a clean, even cut and a stable item weight — critical when you sell sausage rolls at volume and calculate cost per portion.

For a bakery or a frozen-pastry producer this means a predictable result on every batch: the same weight, the same look on the display case and no waste from a burst sausage. We supply the sausage wholesale separately — you use your own dough and recipe — or you take the finished item whole.

How to choose a wholesale sausage-roll supplier

When you choose where to buy sausage rolls wholesale, price per piece is not the only criterion. For a bakery, gas station or chain, predictability matters more: stable item weight, the same look on the display case and unchanging behaviour during finishing. If the sausage bursts in the dough or the dough goes soggy, you get waste, unhappy guests and a "floating" cost. So first check how the sausage behaves inside the dough when baked, and use a sausage made specifically for the job — casing-free.

The second criterion is shelf life and storage format. A ready-made frozen sausage roll with a 360-day shelf life lets you keep stock and finish in batches on demand, without a daily dependence on deliveries. This is especially valuable for gas-station chains, canteens and corporate catering with uneven flow. If you have your own bakery and dough, it is better to take the separate sausage for assembly wholesale.

The third criterion is halal and documentation. For retail, marketplaces and chain foodservice you need an EAEU declaration, HACCP/ISO 22000:2018 and a valid Halal certificate. We produce wholesale halal sausage rolls from poultry and beef with a full document package and Mercury/Vetis records, which removes risk when entering chains and platforms.
