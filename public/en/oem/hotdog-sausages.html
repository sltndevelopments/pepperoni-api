FAQ

Are the hot-dog sausages halal?
Yes, all sausages are 100% halal: beef, poultry, lamb. Halal certificate from the Spiritual Board of Muslims of Tatarstan #614A/2024 and #884A/2025, HACCP, ISO 22000:2018.
Do you produce hot-dog sausages wholesale under our brand?
Yes, we are a hot-dog sausage supplier under private label: recipe, caliber, length and packaging to your fuel-station or street-food chain. Wholesale from 500 kg/mo.
Are the sausages suitable for a roller grill at fuel stations?
Yes. They are thermostable, do not shrink and hold their shape on a roller grill, pan or grate — designed for fuel stations, food courts and street food.
What is the MOQ and lead time?
Pilot from 500 kg/mo, serial 2–5 t/mo. 4–8 weeks brief to shipment, sample in 2–3 weeks.
Where do you ship?
EXW Kazan and a Lyubertsy warehouse near Moscow, shipping across Russia and export to CIS and the UAE.
