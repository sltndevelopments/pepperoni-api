How to choose a wholesale burger patty supplier

When you decide where to buy burger patties wholesale, price per kilogram is not the only criterion. For a chain or HoReCa operation, predictability matters more: a stable patty weight batch to batch, a consistent diameter to fit your bun, and unchanging cooking behaviour. A swing of just 5–7 grams across a high-volume line means a "floating" cost per portion and guest complaints about inconsistent burger size. So first ask the producer for weight and diameter tolerances and lock them into the specification.

The second criterion is heat stability and shrinkage. Pre-cooked halal patties should not lose more than 8–10% of their weight when reheated and must hold their shape on the grill and contact surface. Raw formed burger patties give a "made on site" freshness but demand strict cold-chain discipline. We supply both formats and help you pick the one that fits your equipment and serving speed.

The third criterion is documentation and halal. For retail, marketplaces and HoReCa you need an EAEU declaration, HACCP/ISO 22000:2018 and a valid Halal certificate. All our burger patties are made from halal beef and poultry, with a full document package and Mercury/Vetis veterinary records, removing risk when listing with chains and platforms.

Wholesale terms, logistics and portion economics

Wholesale supply of burger patties is built around your volume. A pilot of 500 kg per month is enough to test the patty in your menu and gather feedback. Moving to a 2–5 t/month series, we fix the price and delivery schedule, protecting your cost base from market swings. We ship EXW Kazan and from Kazan — across Russia and for export to the CIS and the UAE, blast-frozen with full cold-chain control.

To calculate the economics, start from the patty weight: with a price per kilogram and a fixed weight you get the exact cost of the meat part of the burger. A calibrated patty removes hidden losses on over- and under-weight, and a stable diameter eliminates waste from bun mismatch. Under private label we tune weight (100/150/200 g), diameter, recipe and packaging — from interleaving and flow-pack to a corrugated case with your design.
