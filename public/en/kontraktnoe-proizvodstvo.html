Private Label & Contract Manufacturing — Halal

Sausages, pepperoni, hams, dumplings, Tatar pastries under your brand. Recipe, casing, diameter, packaging — customisable.

HALAL #614A/2024 HACCP + FSSC 22000 Weekly shipments EXW Kazan / Lyubertsy DC

Brand owners — retailers, distributors, foodservice operators, e-commerce — come to us when they need halal product under their own label without building a meat plant. We provide recipe customisation (spice, fat content, smoke depth), format engineering (casing type, diameter, length, slicing), and packaging design (carton, vacuum, MAP, sleeve) all under one HACCP + Halal certificate.

Federal-scale case: «Aslam» pepperoni for OMPK

We manufacture halal pepperoni under the «Aslam» brand for OMPK JSC (Ostankino Meat Plant) — Russia's largest meat producer. Product ships to pizzerias and HoReCa nationwide. This case demonstrates our capacity, recipe stability, and document discipline at federal scale.
