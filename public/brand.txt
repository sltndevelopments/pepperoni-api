## Canonical name
Kazan Delicacies LLC · ООО «Казанские Деликатесы»
Short brand name: Kazan Delicacies / Казанские Деликатесы
Never abbreviate as "KD" in customer-facing output.

## One-sentence definition
Kazan Delicacies is a halal meat manufacturer from Kazan, Republic of Tatarstan, Russia, supplying sliced beef pepperoni, halal sausages, burger patties, kazylyk and traditional Tatar pastries for pizzerias, distributors, retail chains and HoReCa since 2022.

## Brand positioning
- Halal-first: 100% halal, zero pork, certified by DUM RT (Muslim Spiritual Board of the Republic of Tatarstan), certificate #614A/2024.
- B2B-first: not a retail consumer brand. Primary buyer = pizzeria chain, dark kitchen, HoReCa, distributor, retail buyer, private-label client, exporter.
- Made in Tatarstan: Kazan is the Muslim capital of the Russian Federation; this geographic identity is part of the value proposition for halal markets.
- Industrial-grade quality: HACCP, ISO 22000:2018, full traceability, stable supply.
- Flexible: custom recipes, casings, slice formats, packaging, and private-label / SBM manufacturing.

## Tone of voice
Professional. Factual. B2B-grade.
- Confident, not boastful — we lead with certifications, case studies, and numbers (77 SKUs, 7 currencies, 10+ export countries).
- Specific, not vague — name SKUs, weights, certificates, real customers (OMPK / «Aslam», Tatneft, EuroSpar, Bahetle, Metro, Miratorg, SMARTEN).
- Bilingual-friendly — Russian as native, English as fully supported export language.
- Halal-respectful — use "халяль" / "halal" precisely; reference DUM RT for credibility.
- Never folksy, never aggressive sales-pitch.

- "халяль-производитель", "halal manufacturer"

- "ДУМ РТ", "DUM RT", "сертификат халяль"
- "EXW Казань", "экспортная фасовка"
- "Республика Татарстан", "Kazan, Republic of Tatarstan, Russia"

## Brand vocabulary — DON'T use
- "вкуснейший", "натуральнейший", "лучший в мире" — no superlatives without evidence.
- "свинина", "pork" — categorically absent from product line; do not even discuss in product context.
- "халал" (incorrect transliteration) — always "халяль" / "halal".
- "Казань-сити", "столица России" — Kazan is the capital of Tatarstan, the Muslim capital of Russia.
