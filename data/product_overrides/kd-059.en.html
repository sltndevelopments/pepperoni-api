Storage and Logistics for «Губадия с кортом»

«Губадия с кортом» is shipped frozen to maintain peak freshness and texture. Upon arrival, it should be stored at -18°C (0°F) for a shelf life of 12 months from the production date. For immediate use, thaw the product in a refrigerator at 4°C (40°F) for 8–10 hours before serving. Once thawed, the pie can be held refrigerated for up to 5 days in its original sealed packaging. For hot service, reheat in a preheated oven at 160°C (320°F) for 10–12 minutes, or microwave individual portions for 30–45 seconds. The product is packed in individual sealed bags within corrugated cases of 6 or 12 units. Each case is labeled with batch number, production date, and halal certification mark. Logistics partners include refrigerated trucking for LTL and FTL shipments, with expedited options available for time-sensitive orders. We recommend storing away from strong-smelling foods to prevent odor absorption.
