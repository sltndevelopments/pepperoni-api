Storage, Logistics, and Shelf Life Guidelines

Proper storage and logistics are critical to maintaining the quality of Turkey Ham in a Loaf. The product must be kept at a consistent temperature of 0°C to 4°C (32°F to 39°F) throughout the cold chain. Under these conditions, the unopened loaf has a shelf life of 45 days from the production date. Once opened, it should be consumed within 5 days or vacuum-sealed for extended use. For frozen storage, the loaf can be kept at -18°C (0°F) for up to 6 months, though thawing should be done gradually in refrigeration to preserve texture. Logistics partners must provide temperature-monitored transport with data loggers; we require a 24-hour advance notice for deliveries to ensure proper cold storage readiness at the receiving facility. Damaged or temperature-abused shipments must be reported within 48 hours for replacement eligibility.
