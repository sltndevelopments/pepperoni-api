Storage, Logistics, and Shelf Life

«Перемяч жареный» is a frozen product requiring consistent storage at -18°C or below to maintain quality and safety. Under optimal conditions, the shelf life is 12 months from the production date. For logistics, we ship via refrigerated trucks to major distribution hubs in Russia and CIS countries, with international shipping available through certified cold chain partners. Upon arrival, products should be transferred immediately to freezer storage and kept frozen until preparation. Thawing is not recommended; instead, reheat directly from frozen in a preheated oven at 180°C for 8–10 minutes or deep fry at 170°C for 3–4 minutes for best results. Proper handling ensures the pastry’s crispy exterior and moist filling are preserved, minimizing food safety risks.
