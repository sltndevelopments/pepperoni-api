Storage & Logistics: Handling Сервелат Ханский

Proper storage and logistics are critical to maintain the quality of Сервелат Ханский, a smoked sausage sensitive to temperature and humidity. The product must be stored at 0°C to +4°C in its original vacuum-sealed packaging to prevent moisture loss and oxidation. Once opened, it should be consumed within 48 hours if refrigerated, or frozen at -18°C for up to 6 months without significant texture degradation. For logistics, we recommend refrigerated transport with temperature monitoring; the product can withstand short temperature excursions (up to +8°C for 4 hours) without spoilage, but continuous cold chain is advised. Each case (10 kg net weight) is packed in corrugated boxes with internal dividers to prevent crushing. For international shipments, we arrange reefer containers with proper ventilation and traceability. Our logistics team provides real-time tracking for all wholesale orders, and we offer consolidated shipping for multiple SKUs to reduce per-unit freight costs. For private-label clients, we can customize packaging dimensions to fit specific retail shelf or foodservice storage requirements, including smaller case sizes (5 kg) for HoReCa outlets with limited cooler space.
