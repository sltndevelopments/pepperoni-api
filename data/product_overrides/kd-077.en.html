Storage and Logistics: Shelf Life and Distribution

«Маффин шоколадный» is stored at ambient temperature (15-25°C) with a shelf life of 45 days from production date. Avoid direct sunlight and high humidity to maintain texture and prevent mold. For logistics, each muffin is individually sealed in a food-grade polypropylene wrap, then packed in corrugated cardboard boxes with inner dividers to prevent crushing. Pallet configuration: 48 boxes per pallet (1,152 muffins), net weight ~70 kg per pallet. We ship via refrigerated truck only during summer months (above 30°C) to prevent chocolate bloom; standard dry van is acceptable year-round in temperate climates. Export shipments use vacuum-sealed master bags inside boxes for extra moisture protection.
