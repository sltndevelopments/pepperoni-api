Storage and Logistics for Премиум Казылык

Премиум Казылык is a shelf-stable dry-cured product with a 12-month shelf life from production date when stored correctly. Optimal storage conditions are 2–8°C (35–46°F) in a cool, dry environment away from direct sunlight and strong odors. The vacuum-sealed packaging maintains freshness without refrigeration for up to 6 months if kept below 20°C, but we recommend chilled storage for maximum quality. Once opened, consume within 5 days. For logistics, the product ships in insulated cartons with gel packs for temperature-sensitive routes (air freight) or standard corrugated boxes for sea freight in climate-controlled containers.
