Storage and Logistics: Handling «Сосиска в тесте» for Maximum Shelf Life

The product must be stored at -18°C or below from receipt to preparation. Shelf life is 12 months from production date when kept continuously frozen. Do not refreeze after thawing. For best results, reheat directly from frozen: 3–4 minutes in a 180°C convection oven, or 60–90 seconds in a 1200W microwave. Avoid deep frying, as the dough’s yeast structure can absorb excess oil. Logistics: we ship via refrigerated truck (temperature-controlled at -20°C) from our Kazan facility. Standard delivery zones include the Russian Federation, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, and select Middle Eastern markets (UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia) via sea freight in insulated containers. For European buyers, we consolidate orders for monthly LCL shipments to Rotterdam or Hamburg. Each pallet holds 48 cartons (960 units) and weighs approximately 480 kg. We provide temperature data loggers for all international shipments at no extra cost. In case of temperature abuse (product exposed to >-12°C for over 2 hours), reject the shipment and contact our logistics team within 48 hours for replacement.
